Makale Başlığı: A case of glomus tumor with mucinous changes
Özet: Aim: Glomus tumors are uncommon lesions especially found on fingertips. Mucinous change in these tumors is a rare phenomenon. In this report we aim to discuss some general and rare features of glomus tumors by a case presentation
