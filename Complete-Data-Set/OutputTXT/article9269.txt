Makale Başlığı: Anesthesia Management of Pregnant with HELLP Syndrome with Fetal Intrauterine Exitus
Özet: HELLP syndrome is a serious cause of mortality for pregnant women and requires careful anesthesia management. In this case, anesthesia management and intensive care clinic of a pregnant woman with intrauterine exitus due to preeclampsia and HELLP syndrome is presented.
