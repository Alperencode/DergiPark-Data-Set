Makale Başlığı: Tocilizumab-Associated Ventricular Tachycardia Case in a COVID-19 Patient
Özet: COVID-19 (Coronavirus Disease-2019) which is dependent on SARS-CoV-2 (Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome) virus started as a pandemic which affected every sphere of life. The disease progresses more heavily and higher mortality is observed in the COVID-19 patients who had cardiovascular disease before or cardiovascular risk factors (smoking habit, male gender, old age, obesity, diabetes, hyperlipidemia and hypertension).  Both COVID-19 disease and the drugs used during treatment lead to cardiac arrhythmia. In this study, a case that was followed up in intensive care unit because of COVID-19 disease and had ventricular tachycardia following Tocilizumab treatment was presented.
