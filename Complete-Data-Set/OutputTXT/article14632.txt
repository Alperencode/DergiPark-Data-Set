Makale Başlığı: Contributions of the First İmam Hatip Generation Pioneers to the Religious Life in Turkey
Özet: The topic of this research is to examine the contributions of the first generation that studied in the İmam Hatip Schools that was opened for the second time in 1951 to the religious life of Turkey. In this research, contributions of the first İmam Hatip generation to the religious life are handled in the context of, services done in the name of İmam Hatip Schools and Higher Islamic Institutes, organisational and publication works pioneered by the first İmam Hatip generation, studies done in scientific and cultural areas, and the domestic and international services done within Presidency of Religious Affairs. In this work which is influenced by relevant literature in determining the names of people who are topic of discussion, biographies, works in which they pen their views and experiences, and topics present in works written about them are examined thematically, and the headlines are determined based on that. Some results of the research are as follows, first İmam Hatip generation has had several varied services in names of İmam Hatip Schools and Higher Islamic Institutes. They organised with organisations such as Talebe Societies, Nesil Movement, Ensar Foundation, and Turkish Religious Foundation to collaborate and speed up their works. They performed publishing activities with various magazines and publishing houses such as İslam'ın İlk Emri Oku, Tohum, Nesil, and İslam Medeniyeti. They performed important duties in social, scientific, cultural, and religious fields with varying tasks.
