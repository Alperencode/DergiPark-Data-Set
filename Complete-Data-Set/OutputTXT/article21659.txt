Makale Başlığı: Breast Feeding is Vital Right for Baby and Mother
Özet: Breastfeeding is essential for a mammalian, Human being, Growth and DevelopmentFor growth and development of an infant/baby as a mammalian species, the Right of Life andvitality is established by breastfeeding, thus, more than a nutrition. This is the indication of theBREASETFEFEDING is honored, esteem act as at this Declaration.At the 1st 1000 Days Congress at 1917, meeting, The Innocenti Declaration, based thebreastfeeding is a part of support and care, thus as meaning innocent, not guilty, natural up tothis act is the Breastfeeding is Honorable Act, as indicating honor of a mother, as mother andinfant/baby relation, natural right, the meaning is humanistic and importance for generalperspective.
