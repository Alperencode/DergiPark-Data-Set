Makale Başlığı: Clinical Characteristics of Monkeypox
Özet: Monkeypox is a zoonotic disease caused by the Monkeypox virus, which belongs to the Orthopoxvirus genus of the Poxviridae family. It exhibits a clinical condition characterized by fever, fatigue, malaise, headache, rash and lymphopathy. Monkeypox is a self-limited illness with symptoms usually lasting 2-4 weeks. In addition, severe cases may occur. The aim of this study is to review the clinical features of monkeypox.
