Makale Başlığı: Abandonment of Trade and Crime of Abandoning Trade
Özet: In Article 44 of the Execution and Bankruptcy Law, the obligations of the merchant who left the trade, in Article 337/a, as a sanction for breach of these obligations imprisonment are regulated. This study aims to evaluate and explain these regulations in the light of judicial decisions. Within  this framework, primarily the obligations of the trader who left the trade, then the prison sentence foreseen as the sanction of breach of these obligations and the burden of proof regulated in this context are explained.
