Makale Başlığı: Frenet Frames of Trigonometric B'ezier Curves
Özet: The geometry of curves and surfaces plays a very important role in computer-aided geometric design (CAGD). The goal of this paper is to construct the Frenet frames of trigonometric B\'ezier curves in Euclidean $ 2 $ and $ 3- $space. Especially, the curvatures of these curves are investigated at the beginning and the ending points.
