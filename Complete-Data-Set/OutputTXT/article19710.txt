Makale Başlığı: THE IMPORTANCE OF INTELLECTUAL CAPITAL IN FAMILY COMPANIES
Özet: Family companies are types of companies created to prevent family-owned assets from going out of the family or to meet the financing needs of family members. In the world, family companies occupy an important position among the types of companies and guide the economy. Nowadays, the structure of society is turning into an information society and new concepts are appearing within this society. One of these concepts, intellectual capital, refers to abstract values, the existence of which is not perceived by the senses, and which puts the concept of knowledge at its cornerstone. It contributes to the continuity of companies in the long term and provides great importance in the competitive arena. In order to gain dominance in this arena, the need for intellectual capital is great. In this study, the aim is to try to explain the importance of intellectual capital in family companies, which occupy an important position among companies in our country, by examining the studies completed in advance.
