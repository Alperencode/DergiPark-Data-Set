Makale Başlığı: THE ROLE OF THE MEDIA IN THE DEMOCRATIZATION OF THE STATES AND THE PROCESSES OF INTEGRATION THE CASE OF KOSOVO
Özet: In this article, dataregarding the role and impact of the media in the integration processes fromcountries in Southeast Europe will be presented. Here, some of the most majorpremises of the obligations from the countries of the Balkans in the fulfillmentof the criteria to be part of the European space will be specified, such as:mechanisms in the prevention and combat of corruption, organized crime, stableeconomy and free trade, freedom of human rights and ethnic minorities, freedomof press and free movement, liberal and comprehensive constitution, rule oflaw, democratic elections and governance. In all these processes, theirreporting and reflection from the media is undoubtedly influential, whichhighlights and attributes to the public opinion the fulfillment and stumble ofthese determining factors of the governments from the countries in SoutheastEurope, in order of being part of the European mechanisms.
