Makale Başlığı: OZAN’DAN ÇAĞDAŞ BİR MESNEVÎ “MARIE SOPHIE”
Özet: In this study, after giving an overview of verse form of mesnevi, we will try to explain why we describe as a modern mesnevi the book named "Marie Sophie" writen by Serkan Ozan Özağaç. In the light of the interview with Özağaç we will determine the main lines of the poet's literary personality. We will construe poetry in the book "Marie Sophie on the presence or absence of" according to poet’s poetica
