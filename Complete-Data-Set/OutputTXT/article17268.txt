Makale Başlığı: RESILIENCE AND LIFE SATISFACTION AS PREDICTORS OF INTERNET ADDICTION IN ADOLESCENTS
Özet: The aim of this study is to examine to what extent resilience and life satisfaction variables predict internet addiction levels of adolescents. The research group consisted of 348 adolescents. The mean age of the research group is 16.37. “Internet Addiction Scale”, “Child and Youth Resilience Measure”, “Life Satisfaction Scale” and “Personal Information Form” were used as data collection tools. In the study, the data were analyzed with the multiple linear regression analysis (stepwise) method. As a result of the research, it was determined that the variables of resilience and life satisfaction predicted internet addiction in adolescents at a significant level.
