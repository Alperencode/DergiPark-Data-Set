Makale Başlığı: Marmaris Declaration: Ethical Concepts of Right to life and Pre-viability
Özet: Ethical principles are mainly constructed under the “human rights” concepts, and ethics as aphilosophical aspect under medical perspective, try to find the answer of “what is right” and“ought to do” for the special condition or case/patient. Consider or treat the human not thedisease will be our aim with empathic approach.
