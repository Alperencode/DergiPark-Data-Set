Makale Başlığı: Attitudes of Nursing Students Towards Ageism
Özet: Objective: This study was carried out in order to determine the perspective of the students studying in the nursing department towards ageism and to determine how their attitudes changed with the determined variables.Material and Methods: The research was conducted with 1744 nursing students who agreed to participate and whose consents were obtained online. While collecting the study data, "Demographic data form" and "Age discrimination attitude scale" were used.Results: Elderly discrimination attitude scale average score and gender, class, presence of elderly people in need of care in their environment; Between the average score of the Limitation of the Life of the Elderly sub-dimension and gender, the presence of an elderly person in the family; There are statistically significant differences between the mean score of the Negative Discrimination sub-dimension and the class, the presence of the elderly in need of care, the place where the elderly person in the family lives, and the presence of an elderly person in the family (p <0.05).Conclusion: In our study, fourth grade students displayed a positive attitude compared to other groups, and this result was found to be statistically significant. In our study, it was concluded that students' attitudes towards the elderly were positive.
