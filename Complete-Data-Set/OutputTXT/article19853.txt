Makale Başlığı: Acute Femoral Artery Occlusion and Lower Extremity Ischemia in a Patient with COVID-19: A Case Report
Özet: Clinical spectrum of COVID-19 disease; it ranges from asymptomatic infection to severe disease with hypoxaemic respiratory failure, septic shock and multi-organ dysfunction requiring invasive mechanical ventilation and other organ support in intensive care units. The presence of hypercoagulation, which is characterized by micro and macro vascular thrombotic angiopathy, has been shown to serious and critical conditions in COVID-19 patients. In this case; lower extremity ischemia due to acute femoral artery occlusion during follow-up in the intensive care unit due to severe COVID-19 disease is presented.
