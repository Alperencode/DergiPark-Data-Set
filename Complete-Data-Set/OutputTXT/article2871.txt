Makale Başlığı: Personal Health Information Systems for Health Tourism Patients
Özet: The work "Personal Health Information Systems for Health Tourism Patients" was published in 2021 by Dr. Banu Fulya YILDIRIM. This book  Ankara University Institute of Social Sciences, Department of Information and Records Management Faculty Member Prof. Dr. Fahrettin ÖZDEMİRCİ and Hacettepe University Medical Pharmacology Department Lecturer Assoc. Dr. Güray SOYDAN. It is the book version of the doctoral thesis titled "Developing E-Pulse Applications as an Information System for Health Tourism Patients: A Model Proposal" prepared under the consultancy.
