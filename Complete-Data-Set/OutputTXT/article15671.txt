Makale Başlığı: AN EVALUATİON OF ARTİFİCİAL INTELLİGENCE JOURNALİSM AND JOURNALİSM DİSCUSSİONS
Özet: Technological developments have produced socio-economic consequences in any period and at any level. In terms of the age we live in, the importance of digitalization that changes our lives is an undeniable fact. Journalism, which is an indisputable part of society and financial resources, also takes its share from these developments. It is no longer looked at who did the news, but what might have done it. Because digital technology has produced artificial intelligence and it is frequently encountered in the field of journalism. Moreover, artificial intelligence journalism claims to replace the journalist. These developments reveal new debates in terms of journalism profession and news production. These discussions have a very broad framework. The aim of the study is to draw a portrait of what kind of issues the developing interaction between artificial intelligence journalism and journalism raises. For this reason, developments in artificial intelligence journalism will be evaluated within the framework of journalism, news and content production in this study. In addition, the debates caused by artificial intelligence journalism in terms of journalism will be discussed.
