Makale Başlığı: A Study on Horadam Hybrid Numbers
Özet: In this paper, we study Horadam hybrid numbers. For these numbers, we give the exponential generating function, Poisson generating function, generating matrix, Vajda's, Catalan's, Cassini's, and d'Ocagne's identities. In addition, we offer Honsberger formula, general bilinear formula, and some summation formulas for these numbers.
