Makale Başlığı: THE RELATIONSHIP OF ART EDUCATION AND ART PRODUCTS WITH THE ECONOMY IN A GLOBALIZED WORLD
Özet: In this study, document analysis was used as one of the qualitative research methods in order to examine the relationship between art education and art products in a globalized world.The data of the study were obtained and examined as a result of a large-area studying In the globalized world, the economy is one of the most important areas and affects many areas as well. When the relationship of the economy with art education and art products was examined, it was seen that they were important factors affecting one another. It is especially noteworthy that economics and art are the elements that develop and reproduce one another. Since a qualified art education will turn into qualified art products, the role of education is undeniable. In addition, the provision of state support for art education and the production of art is also very important in terms of not compromising the quality of art.
