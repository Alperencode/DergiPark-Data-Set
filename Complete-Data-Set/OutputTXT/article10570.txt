Makale Başlığı: Religion and Spirituality in Spiritual Problems Case Studies for Mental Health Professionals, Spiritual Support Professionals and Everyone
Özet: The book titled Religion and Spirituality in Spiritual Problems was written by Psychiatrist Okan İmre and delivered to the readers through Kitaparası Yayınları in 2022. The book called Religion and Spirituality in Spiritual Problems, which takes reference from 39 sources and consists of 126 pages; It consists of a total of 5 sections: What is Religion?, What is Psychotherapy?, How is the Situation in Our Country?, Integration of Spirituality and Some Case Examples. The book also includes a foreword and a bibliography.
