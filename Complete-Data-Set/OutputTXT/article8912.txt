Makale Başlığı: CHARACTERIZATIONS OF CONTACT PSEUDO-SLANT SUBMANIFOLDS OF A PARA-KENMOTSU MANIFOLD
Özet: In this paper, the geometry of contact pseudo-slant submanifolds of a para Kenmotsu manifoldhowe been studied. The necessary and sufficient conditions for a submanifolds to be a contact pseudoslantsubmanifolds of a para Kenmotsu manifold are given.
