Makale Başlığı: Solution of KdV and Boussinesq using Darboux Transformation
Özet: Two Darboux transformations of the Korteweg-de Vries (KdV) equation and Boussinesq equation are constructed throughthe Darboux method. Soliton solutions of these two equations are presented by applying the Darboux transformations.
