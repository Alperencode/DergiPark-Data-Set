Makale Başlığı: THE RELATIONSHIP OF COVID-19 RELATED ANXIETY WITH POSITIVE AND NEGATIVE EMOTIONS OF INDIVIDUALS
Özet: Covid-19 has emerged as a pandemic that has affected all humanity since the end of 2019 and has affected the mental health of individuals, causing anxiety, fear, uncertainty, and hopelessness. This study aimed to examine the relationship between anxiety caused by Covid-19 and positive and negative emotions.3121 people with an average age of 31.3 (SD=10.20) were included in this cross-sectional study conducted online.
