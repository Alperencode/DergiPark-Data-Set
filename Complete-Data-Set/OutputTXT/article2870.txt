Makale Başlığı: The SAGE Handbook of Social Media Research Methods
Özet: In our era, social media is accepted as an environment that has great importance in human life. The great importance of social media in human life has enabled it to be used in many areas. As a result, social media has become a data-generating place and research tool that researchers can use in their academic studies. However, this new type of data has brought many challenges for researchers. In order to alleviate some of these difficulties and to guide researchers who want to use social media data in their studies, the book “The SAGE Handbook of Social Media Research Methods”, which provides a detailed view of the field of social media research consisting of 39 different studies, has been prepared under the editorship of Luke Sloan and Anabel Quan-Haas. In this study, these studies in the book have been strived to be reviewed.
