Makale Başlığı: Nursing Care in A Child with Leigh Syndrome: A Case Report
Özet: Leigh syndrome which affects the central nervous system, is a rare inherited neurometabolic disorder. It usually occurs apparent in the first year of life. Patients with Leigh syndrome are at high risk for respiratory impairment, problems with growth and cognitive development, encephalopathy, hypotonia, spasticity, epilepsy and convulsion. The neurologic manifestations may begin in infancy or early childhood, progressively worsen, and eventually lead to death in early childhood. There is no specific treatment for the disease. Early detection of this life-threatening disease, prevention of possible complications, and evidence-based nursing care for clinical findings are the most important factors that increase the quality of life of patients. In this case, the nursing care of the baby diagnosed with Leigh syndrome is presented.
