Makale Başlığı: Level of Learned Resourcefulness of Nursing Students and Associated Factors
Özet: Objective: This study was carried out to determine thelevel of learned resourcefulness of nursing students andtheir relationship with sociodemographic variables.Method: The study was conducted in a descriptiverelationship-seeking type and the students studying at thenursing faculty of a university located in the city centerof Konya constituted the universe of the study. Aminimum of 261 students formed the study sample,according to the calculation of the sample size of whichthe universe is known. The study was completed with 289students who agreed to participate in the study. "PersonalInformation Form" and "Rosenbaum LearnedResourcefulness Scale" were used to collect data. Mean,standard deviation, independent groups t test, one wayanova test and pearson correlation were used forstatistical analysis.Results: The mean learned resourcefulness score of thenursing students was found to be 117.50±17.75. Nostatistically significant difference was found between thestudents' sociodemographic variables and the learnedresourcefulness score average (p>0.05).Usage in Practice: It was found that the mean learnedresourcefulness score of nursing students was moderateand there was no significant relationship betweensociodemographic characteristics and learnedresourcefulness level. It is recommended that preventiveprograms and courses to increase the learnedresourcefulness level of nursing students should be addedto the curriculum.
