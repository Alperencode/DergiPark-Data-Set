Makale Başlığı: CHANGE IN THE NOTION OF SECURITY AND ENVIRONMENTAL SECURITY
Özet: The aim of the study is to explain the transformation of security in terms of environment by analyzing environment security relation with regards to globalization. To achieve that, new security perspective is explained along with the terms of security and environment and environmental security is described after the subject of security is analyzed. The rapid change all around the world has caused changes on perspectives on global security problems. The concept of security is no more just a subject of national security, but has become an international subject with the rise of environmental problems.
