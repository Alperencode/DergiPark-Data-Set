Makale Başlığı: A The Historical Development of Childhood and Children's Rights
Özet: Among the stages of life childhood has a special and decisive influence on human development. At this stage, when a person starts to understand himself, the people around him, the opportunities he/she can't reach and the relationship between them, the opportunities he/she has/can't have, maybe contain causes and results that will be effective throughout his whole life. These most vulnerable years of human life appear as one of the most sensitive periods for a person to realize himself. The meaning and function attributed to the child has a history that develops in parallel with the importance and value given to human existence. The lessons learned from these experiences by people who realize what problems they have to struggle with and what consequences they have to face in their lives since childhood, in what we can call adulthood, are of great value for children and humanity. This study aims to present a holistic framework with a Turkey focus on the historical development of the phenomenon of childhood and children's rights. It has been tried to open a window on the lesson that needs to be learned in order to develop children and their rights from past to present
