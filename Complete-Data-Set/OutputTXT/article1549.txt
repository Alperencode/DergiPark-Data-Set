Makale Başlığı: A Qualitative Research of the Business Operators’ Perception and Attitudes towards Tourism: The Example of Odunpazarı
Özet: Tourism, has affected the Odunpazarı protected area in various dimensions. The effects have socio-cultural, economic and environmental dimensions. The most affected stakeholders by these effects are the operators in the area. Therefore, in this study it is aimed to determine the perceptions and attitudes of the business operators in the Odunpazarı protected area regarding the effects of tourism. Qualitative research method has been used in the research. Semi-structured interviews have been conducted face-to face with 21 business operators in the field. The data obtained has been analyzed by descriptive and content analysis method. As a result of the study, it has been determined that the operators in the field believed that tourism ensures the protection of cultural heritage and economic development.
