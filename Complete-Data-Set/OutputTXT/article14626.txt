Makale Başlığı: Program Development Works At The Board Of Education And Discipline (1946-1973)
Özet: The President of Board of Education and Discipline, which has been working as an important decision-making body of the Ministry of National Education since 1926, takes place at the beginning of instutions, dominating the Turkish National Education. This study was prepared to explain the work the Ministry had done in the field of program development from 1946 to 1973.
