Makale Başlığı: Civil Society organizations' role in Early recovery in Northwest Syria
Özet: In the light of the increasing humanitarian needs and the extending humanitarian crisis, it was necessary to look for more sustainable and efficient solutions to meet the humanitarian needs. Early recovery (ER) in northwest Syria was a complex file because of the possible overlapping with the issue of reconstruction which is considered a highly sensitive political issue.  in this complicated context, Civil society organizations (CSOs) played an important role in the North-west of Syria (NWS) early recovery in several dimensions and this article discussing this role and its prospects shortly.
