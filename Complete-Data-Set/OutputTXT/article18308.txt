Makale Başlığı: SOCIO-ECONOMIC AND DEMOGRAPHIC STATUS OF SABAH NEIGHBORHOOD IN USAK DISTRICT ACCORDING TO THE TAX ROLL WITH THE REGISTER NO. 17617 (1844-1845)
Özet: This paper has been prepared in the light of the Tax roll (Temettuat defteri) dated 1844-1845, which is registered in the Ottoman Archives of Presidency of State Archives with the base of ML. VRD. TMT. d. 17617. In this context, the socio-economic and demographic structure of the Sabah neighborhood (Islam) within the Usak district in the first half of the 19th century was discussed in the period examined. With the study, the demographic structure of the residents of Sabah (Islam) Neighborhood in the middle of the 19th century, the income and taxes paid by the household heads as a result of their activities related to agriculture, animal farming, labor, tradesmanship, and trade were specified. Moreover, data such as the estimated population of the Sabah neighborhood, the number of agricultural lands, the diversity of agricultural production, the type and number of cattle and small cattle and pack animals in the neighborhood were compiled in the light of the Tax roll on the examined date (1844- 1845). In addition, occupations performed in the same neighborhood, nicknames, names, and titles used by people are among the subjects included in the study.
