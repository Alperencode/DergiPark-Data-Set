Makale Başlığı: FATIMID-BYZANTINE POLITICAL RELATIONS (971-1069)
Özet: This research was meant to describe the political relationship between the Byzantine and Fatimid states. Due to the influence of the Fatimids extended to Egypt during the reign of the Fatimid Caliph Muizz Li-Dinillah, they tried to return the cities that the Byzantines had conquered in the north of the Levant. The effort was made by the Fatimid leader Jaafar bin Falah, as he prepares alarge army to return these cities from the Byzantines, including Antakya, but the mission failed to be accomplished.Conflicts and military attacks continued between the Fatimid state and the Byzantine Empire until the year 988, when the messenger of the Byzantine came to Egypt. II. Basileios Came with the gifts to the Fatimid Caliph  Aziz-Billah requesting for a peaceful agreement between the two states, an agreement to stop fighting and attacking each other that last only for limited period and then Byzantines continued to seize opportunities to undermine the Fatimids. The reason why a military confrontations between the two sides were repeated until the end of the Fatimid Caliph Mustansır Billah’s reign , where Many parts and cities of the Levant came out from under his rule, and the influence of the Fatimids receded from the Levant.
