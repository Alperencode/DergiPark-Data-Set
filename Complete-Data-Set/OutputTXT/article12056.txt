Makale Başlığı: Lenın and the Russıan Revolutıon
Özet: Vladimir Ilyich Ulyanov (Lenin) was born in 1870 at Simbirsk on the middle Volga, in Russia. Lenin had a normal middle-class education. He died in January 1924, at the age of fifty-three. The author Christopher Hill (1912-2003) is a British historian. Hill has been considering Lenin as the theorist, organizer and leader of revolt. Barely a quarter of the book to discuss his seven years’ work as the head of the first socialist state in the world.  Lenin and the Russian Revolution examines the background to and the course of the Russian Revolution of 1917 and Lenin's regime. In this book Hill, offers a comprehensive cultural, political, economic and social history of developments in Russia in the 1920's. This study examines the key events in history, Lenin and the Russian Revolution.
