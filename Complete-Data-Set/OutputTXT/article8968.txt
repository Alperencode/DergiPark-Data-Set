Makale Başlığı: At the Center of the World: Robert College
Özet: Do you know how Robert College, which we know that many famous names graduated from, achieved this success and on what foundations it was founded? At this point, Önder Kaya aims to enlighten us, the readers. Önder Kaya's Robert College is a meticulously studied, fluent research work that gives off a novel-like feel while reading. There is no doubt that it will be useful for readers who are specifically interested in the history of the school. The process leading to the establishment of Robert College and its merger with the American College for Girls are discussed in the historical flow. In the work, which describes the conditions that necessitated the birth of Boğaziçi University, you understand why the education of the College is so different from the others.
