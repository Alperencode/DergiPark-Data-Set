Makale Başlığı: An İmportant Motivation Element İn Education: Age İndependence Thought
Özet: With modernization, the importance of education has increased even more. After the understanding of the importance given to education by all of the leading countries in terms of social and economic welfare, the tools that can be used for a quality education have been researched and continue to be researched. Motivation is defined as all the elements that will increase the quality in terms of education.Play is one of the important tools of motivation. Its positive contributions to learning processes, regardless of age, enable it to be used as an important source of motivation. In this context, considering the contribution of the game in terms of development, it is seen that it is an important motivation factor.
