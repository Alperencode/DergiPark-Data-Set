Makale Başlığı: THE EFFECTS OF THE OTTOMAN-AUSTRIAN WARS ON ISTANBUL CASH WAQFS (1693-1697)
Özet: Waqfs played an active role in the economic and social life of the Ottoman Empire. The effects of waqfs, especially in the economic structure, have increased with the spread of cash waqfs, which are a financial institution. Cash waqfs, which were established to meet the credit needs of various segments of the society, have become prominent institutions with their economic aspects.In this study, the accounting records of the cash waqfs registered in the Istanbul Shari records were examined, and information was given about the credit transactions and social services of the waqfs that continued their activities in Istanbul between 1693 and 1697, in the last periods of the Ottoman-Austrian wars. In addition, have been compared. the data in the accounting records of the waqfs that continued their activities during and before the war in Istanbul. The obtained data were subjected to determinations and evaluations, taking into account the socioeconomic structure of the period. In this context; The Ottoman-Austrian wars did not have a serious effect on the financial situation, economic activities and social services of the cash waqfs, It has been determined that waqfs continue their activities without interruption. This situation reveals that cash waqfs were based on solid and well-established foundations in the Ottoman economic structure.
