Makale Başlığı: Use of Newspapers and Internet in Social Studies Textbooks
Özet: In this study, in order to create a culture of global citizenship, to show that the information acquired at school is relevant in social life, and to raise awareness, the use of newspaper and general network news containing current events and topics in social studies textbooks of 2019 was examined. In the research, one of the qualitative research methods, the document analysis method was used. In the research, fourth, fifth, sixth and seventh grade social studies textbooks used in the 2021-2022 academic year were used as data sources. In the study, newspaper and general network news in the textbooks were determined and analyzed according to learning areas, units, classes and years and presented in tables. The findings show that newspaper and general network news are mostly dated 2018, the most used news source is general network news and the least used news source is newspaper news, the most news source is in the "Global Connections" learning area, and the It has been determined that it belongs to the least "Culture and Heritage" learning area. In addition, it was observed that newspaper news was not included in the 6th grade textbook, only the general network news source was included. Based on all these data, some suggestions were made.
