Makale Başlığı: Sekiz yaş grubuyla dramadan oyuna uzanan süreçte oluşturulan bir etkinlik: Hayat Ağacı
Özet: The show with the name of Tree of Life is a performance which is created from the question of “Could a fiction and performance be created with children at the age of 8”. In the process of project, with some improvisations, children’s imagination and ob servations, their thought about civil and children’s rights, war, peace and other rights are revealed. Children’s skills of dance, rhythm and harmony are tested and performed under the supervision of the students of 4th year of Uludağ University Faculty of Fine Arts, Performing Arts- Acting and Playwriting Departments. Datas obtained with impovisations are created by Prof. Dr. Nurhan Tekerek and they are presented in II. Bursa International ShortFilm Festival in Tayyare Culture Center. A great progress is observed at the students of the age of 8 and the students of 4th year in Acting-Department during 6 months of rehearsals and impovisations.
