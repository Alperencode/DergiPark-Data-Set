Makale Başlığı: Cultural Imperialism and 'New Media': The Example of Korean Culture in Turkish Society
Özet: The rapid change in internet technologies and communication tools has brought important changes in the lives of people and societies. Thanks to the Internet, different cultural values circulate quickly and freely all over the world. This situation affects cultural interaction in positive or negative ways. As a result of this cultural interaction, it is among the options to accept cultural imperialism and lose the characteristics of its own culture. In this study, while the effects of the networks that surround us all around us on the cultures of the societies with the spread of the new media are discussed, the effect of the new media in the formation and reproduction of cultural imperialism is examined. In this framework, it is aimed to deal with cultural imperialism and new media phenomena in Turkish society through the example of Korean culture. Thus, the subject of the study is important in terms of having a unique place in the literature.
