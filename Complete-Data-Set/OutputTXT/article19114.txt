Makale Başlığı: Drug Protocols on Prostate Cancer Clinic Studies
Özet: In this review article, used drug protocols on prostate cancer clinical studies. The review cover the possible treatment options include surgery, radiotherapy (RT), hormonal therapy, chemotherapy (CT), immunotherapy or a combination of these depending on the stage of the disease or the medical condition of the patient in prostate cancer cases.
