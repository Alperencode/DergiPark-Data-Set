Makale Başlığı: Coprotus Korf & Kimbr.: A new coprophilous genus record for the mycobiota of Türkiye
Özet: The genus Coprotus Korf & Kimbr, was given as new record for the mycobiota of Türkiye based on the collection and the identification of Coprotus ochraceus (P. Crouan & H. Crouan) J. Moravec from Şirvan district of Siirt province. A brief description of the species is given together its photographs related to its macro and micro morphologies.
