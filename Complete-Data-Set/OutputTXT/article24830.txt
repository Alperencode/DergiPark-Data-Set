Makale Başlığı: The Evaluation of Patient Care Behaviors of Nurses by  Patients Who Apply to Emergency
Özet: Objective: This study is designed to be descriptive for the evaluation of patient care behaviors of nurses as made by the patients who applied to emergency. Method The study was carried out with 106 patients who applied for emergency services between February 01 and June 30, 2016. Socio-Demographic Form and Care Behavior Scale-24 were used as data collection tools. Likert type scale is comprised of 4 sub-scales as assurance, knowledge and skill, respectfulness and commitment.
