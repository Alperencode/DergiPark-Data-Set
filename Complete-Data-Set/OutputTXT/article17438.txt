Makale Başlığı: UDDER MICROBIOTA IN DAIRY COWS
Özet: Existence of microbiota in mammals become a popular research subjectnowadays. The microbiota is present in different parts of the creature, intensively inintestine, skin, vagina and udder. Microbiota has an importance in animal health asit gives information of about diseases and it’s curative roles. Due to microorganismsbeing the major agent in the occurrence of diseases, microbiota should be broadlyevaluated. The aim of this review is constituting a perspective about bacteria inmicrobiota, approaches to detect microbiota, changes in udder microbiota, microbiotain teat and udder canals and colostrum, origin of microbiota, and interrelation withmastitis and microbiota.
