Makale Başlığı: Theory Usage in Research: An Investigation within the Scope of National Tourism Research
Özet: Theory increases scientific understanding through its ability to explain and predict phenomenons, events etc. In order for research to be considered as scientific and qualified, theoretical background is important. Considering the importance of theory in a scientific research, theory usage in the scope of national tourism research aroused curiosity. To better understand a particular discipline, it is necessary to identify the theories that guide research. Based on this, articles in the tourism journals within ULAKBİM TR Index, which were published between 2016 and 2020, were examined by content analysis. It was detected that 171 of 1792 articles include theoretical basis. Furthermore, theories such as Social Exchange Theory, Theory of Planned Behavior, Leadership Theory, Personality Theory, Justice Theory are most frequently used theories in the scope of national tourism research.
