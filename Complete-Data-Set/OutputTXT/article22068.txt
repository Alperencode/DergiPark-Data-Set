Makale Başlığı: EXAMINATION OF ECO-VILLAGES AS AN ALTERNATIVE LIFESTYLE
Özet: The destruction of nature and the perception of unsustainable consumption emerged in the world order that started to change right after the Industrial Revolution took place. In response to this situation it is seen that the defenses based on the idea of protecting the ecological balance have emerged. Between the years 1960-1970 this critical point of view; started to organize in line with the common goal of protecting environmental values. One of the first concrete steps shaped in line with the idea of ecological balance and sustainability that occured in this historical process was the creation of eco-village settlements.Eco-villages; consciously and systematically, its acts with the aim of redesigning natural environments in a sustainable wat by establishing ecological, sociological and psychological balances. Eco-villages; They are full-fledged settlements where human activities are harmlessly integrated with nature which foresees the healthy development of human beings and which can be successfully maintained without interruption. The main idea of eco-villages; is to design the future in a way that respects nature with sustainable constructs.In this study eco-villages as an alternative life style evaluated on the axis of ecology and the foundations of ecological thought eco-village formations and their structural features are discussed through the human nature relationship.
