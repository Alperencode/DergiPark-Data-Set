Makale Başlığı: Respect
Özet: Respect, which is thought to come from the root of counting, is the state of being careful, attentive and measured, based on a mixture of love and shyness towards someone or something, due to its value, superiority, old age, usefulness, and sanctity, and being careful, attentive and measured, reverence, respect, refraining from disturbing others means like feeling. The concept of respect is related to one's self-perception, perspective of the world and events. In order for a person to be respectful to himself and others, he must first have a sense of respect. The quality of the education one receives and the culture in which he lives is also important in the formation and development of this understanding.
