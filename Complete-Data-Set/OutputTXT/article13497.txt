Makale Başlığı: PSEUDOANEURYSM MANAGEMENT IN THE EMERGENCY DEPARTMENT: A CASE REPORT
Özet: Chest pain is among the leading complaints among the reasons for admission to the emergency department and has a wide range of differential diagnosis. Although the diagnostic approach is based on a comprehensive clinical evaluation, it is sometimes extremely difficult to diagnose. In this article, we aimed to review a case of pseudoaneurysm, which is one of the rare causes of chest pain, in the light of the literature.
