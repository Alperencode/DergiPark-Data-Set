Makale Başlığı: A Note on 2-Normed Grand Sequence Spaces
Özet: In this paper, we define 2-normed grand sequence space by inspiration of (Gunawan, 2001) and (Rafeiro et. al., 2018). Also, we give some basic properties of these spaces.
