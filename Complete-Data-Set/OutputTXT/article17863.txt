Makale Başlığı: Satisfaction and Employment Research of Fisheries Technology Graduates: The Case Study of the ÇOMÜ GUBY
Özet: This study aims to examine the employment, career, and satisfaction status of Fisheries Technology graduates with the survey. For this purpose, students who graduated from Çanakkale Onsekiz Mart University Gökçeada School of Applied Sciences, Fisheries Technology Department in 2014-2018; Questions about their age range, gender, whether they chose the department voluntarily, their satisfaction level, whether they worked in any job, their thoughts on whether their field of work is related to the department graduated from, and whether they recommend the department were asked with a questionnaire. Ninety-two people participated in the survey, and the forms were delivered face to face or via social media (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and WhatsApp). According to the survey results, it is understood that the rate of students who willingly come to the department is 34.8%. Similarly, the rate of their willingness to work that graduated from is 28.8%, but most of the participants (71.7%) found a job, albeit in different fields. It is seen that the rate of participants who are satisfied with the department they graduated from (33.7%) and as an effect of this, the rate of participants who recommend the department (29.3%) are similar.
