Makale Başlığı: Generalizations of Different Type Inequalities for $s$-Convex, Quasi-Convex and $P$-Function
Özet: The main purpose of this article is to present the Bullen, Midpoint, Trapezoid and Simpson type inequalities, respectively, for different classes of convexity, with the help of identities existing in the literature.
