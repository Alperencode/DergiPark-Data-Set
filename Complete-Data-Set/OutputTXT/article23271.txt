Makale Başlığı: Book Review: The Transforming Means of Changing Wars, Şirin Duygulu
Özet: Warfare has changed and evolved into a different system as the world order has become more globalized. It should not be forgotten that this transformation has an impact on factors such as actors, tools, time, and space, all of which have an impact on the transformation. In this context, it is also critical to examine the new war tools, as well as the actors and their proxies, in order to comprehend the changing wars. In terms of understanding and interpreting the aforementioned transformation, Şirin Duygulu's book, The Transforming Means of Changing Wars, has made an important contribution to the literature, and its examination has grown in importance.
