Makale Başlığı: Units and 2-class field towers of some multiquadratic number fields
Özet: In this paper, we investigate the unit groups, the 2-class groups, the 2-class field towers and the structures of the second 2-class groups of some multiquadratic number fields of degree 8 and 16.
