Makale Başlığı: The Effect of the Orientation Program Performed for First Year Nursing and Midwifery Students on Their Clinical Stress
Özet: Objective: This study was conducted to determine the effect of an orientation program performed for first year nursing and midwifery students on their clinical stress and to identify their experiences.Methods: The mixed method was used in this study, quasi-experimental and phenomenological type.
