Makale Başlığı: Factors affecting breast milk and the effects of pacifier use on breastfeeding
Özet: Purpose: In this study, we aimed to determine the sociodemographic factors breastfeeding, pacifier use, the problems that may experience in breastfeeding, show effect of pacifier use on breatsfeeding. Material and methods: Interviewing 349 mothers with 24-60-month-old babies who applied to the pediatric outpatient clinics of our hospital for any reason between 2011-2012; A questionnaire consisting of 80 questions was prepared, including patient and parent information, breastfeeding process, and questions for pacifier users. Before the survey Ankara Approval of the Pediatric Health and Diseases Hematology Oncology Training and Research Hospital Ethics Committee was obtained.Results: The breastfeeding rate was 97%. Increasing week of birth, male gender, being first and/or only child, increasing maternal age, fathers’s support for breastfeeding, long maternity leave, increase the duration of breastfeeding; low birth weight, breastfeeding of siblings less than 6 months, high education level of mothers, pregnancy maternal smoking, early initiation of formula, pacifier use adversely affected breastfeeding. The pacifier use was 41.8%, the duration was 18±10.22 months. Pacifier use decreased breastfeeding, it increased the risk of weaning before 6 months by 5.1 times. Conclusion: To increase duration of breastfeeding, mothers should be supperted, early initiation of additional food, formula, pacifier’s negative effects use on breastfeeding should be explained.
