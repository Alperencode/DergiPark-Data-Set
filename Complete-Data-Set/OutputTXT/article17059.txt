Makale Başlığı: OTTOMAN YOUTH ASSOCIATIONS JOURNAL AND THE POLICY OF EDUCATION
Özet: In the 19th century, the Ottoman Empire has made great strides in terms of Westernization with both the Edict of Tanzimat and the declaration of the Constitutional Court. Especially, the power of the press has increased with the proclamation of the II. Constitutional order. Due to the effect of the freedoms granted to the individual, there have also been increases in association activities. During the period of Union and Progress, a number of associations based on the understanding of soldiers/nation were established based on nationalism.One of these associations, the Ottoman Youth Associations started to their activities as a result of the Turkish- German cooperation during the period when the first World War was ongoing. The aim of the association is to enable young people to grow up healthy both physically and spiritually in accordance with the conditions of the period. For this purpose, training, such as physical education, spiritual education, healthy has been given weight. It is aimed to obtain information about the activities of the association by examining what was included in the content of these trainings in the light of the Ottoman Youth Associations assembly.
