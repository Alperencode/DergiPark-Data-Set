Makale Başlığı: Ramadan/Yom Kippur War of 1973 and the Middle East
Özet: Israel’s attempt to raise as a soverign entity on Palestine region in 1948 kick starts one of the most dramatic and problematic phase of the 20th. century history. Because of that unilateral step taken by the Israeli leadership, at least three major wars broke between Israeli and the neighboring Arab states. Israel, which has been in conflict with the Arab countries around it since its participation in the international arena in 1948. Therefore, Israel has securitized and militarized every issues related with the foreign policy and defense realm. The 1973 Yom Kippur War was one of that full scale war which engulfs World Politics in that period, and has serious consequences. The Yom Kippur War and the situation that emerged after the  war has changed Israel defense and foreign policy perceptions. 1973 Yom Kippur War and its major shock changed and modified Israel and Arab States and societies dramatically and destroyed myth of Israel’s invincible state and army.  From this point of view, the political and military situation that emerged after the Yom Kippur War makes the Yom Kippur War important issue both in regional politics and world politics.
