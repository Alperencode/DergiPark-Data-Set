Makale Başlığı: The Turkish Legal Community’s Concern to Duguit: A Literature-Based Assessment
Özet: With his original ideas, Léon Duguit (1859-1928) is one of the foremost and most famous jurist of his time. Duguit’s influence in the legal literature in Turkey seems to be more profound than that of any other jurist. In this study, the works of Léon Duguit in Turkish is evaluated and the persons that are influential in the prevalence of Duguit’s thoughts in Turkey are discussed. Determinations are made on the reasons for this famous jurist’s interest in Turkey.
