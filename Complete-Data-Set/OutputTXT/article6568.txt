Makale Başlığı: Home Advantage in Turkish Handball Super League
Özet: The home advantage in sports is an important issue that has been researched for a long time. In this research, the home advantage of the Turkish Men Handball Super League during the five seasons (2015/2016-2019/2020) was evaluated. In the research, the analysis of variables such as the number of match wins of the home teams and the home advantage according to the seasons were made. In a total of 647 matches played in the five seasons of the Turkish Handball Super League, the home teams won 325 (50.23%) and lost 269 matches (41.57%). The advantage of being the home team in the Handball Super League in five seasons was determined as 53.46%. It is thought that the home advantage is an important factor in sports competitions. It can be said that the good use of the home advantage is a significant factor for the success of a team in the handball branch. It is thought that this situation should be taken into account in the trainings to be made in handball and it can have a significant impact on the success of the team.
