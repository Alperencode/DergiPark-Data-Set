Makale Başlığı: My teacher, Prof. Dr. Tuncer Gulensoy
Özet: Tuncer Gülensoy, in the country; In Ankara, Fırat, Erciyes Universities, abroad, in the USA, Texas Tech; in Kyrgyzstan, Manas; He worked at Girne American Universities in Cyprus. He has published many articles and books in almost every field of Turcology, from Altaicistic and Mongolistic to historical and contemporary dialects of the Turkish language, from etymology to dialects, from onology to folklore, and has had master's and doctoral theses and trained many students. Tuncer Gülensoy, who has published various translated works besides his copyrighted works, is one of the most well-known and well-known professors of Turkish Turcology who educates the most students.He was born on 30 January 1939 in Uşak and died on 11.06.2022 in Ankara.
