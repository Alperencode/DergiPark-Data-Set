Makale Başlığı: The Future of the Syrian Civil War in the Context of Game Theory
Özet: In every period of world history, it is possible to encounter the struggle of groups, clans and nations with each other. These struggles took place sometimes between two different states and sometimes between different groups within the same state. Looking at recent history, an example of these wars that still continues; The Syrian Civil War is shown. The aim of this study is to evaluate the Syrian Civil War within the framework of game theory, based on the strategies of internal and external actors that caused the war. In the preparation phase of the study, literature review was carried out and document analysis method was used. At the same time, the field experiences of the author in the province of Kilis on the Turkey-Syria border line were used. In the first part of the study, the definition of the civil war together with its historical examples, the causes of the Syrian Civil War, its regional and global effects are mentioned. In the second part, the approaches about which side of the war can end with the victory are tried to be evaluated in the context of game theory.
