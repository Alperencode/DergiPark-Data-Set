Makale Başlığı: THE SOVIET INVASION OF AFGHANISTAN AND THE AFGHAN JIHAD IN YEDI GUZEL ADAM’S MAVERA
Özet: Mavera magazine is a literary magazine published in 1976 by a group of poets and writers. The magazine, which stands out in the field of culture and art, is characterized as a type of Islamic literature. Although it is a literary magazine, it publishes on the Middle East, international relations, political issues and notion. The journal closely follows the developments especially in the Islamic world and shares the problems of Muslim peoples with its readers. In this context, the Palestinian problem, the Hama Massacre, other developments in the Islamic geography such as Turkestan and Algeria are the issues that the journal is interested in.The invasion of Afghanistan by the Soviet Union in 1979 and the struggle against the occupation are the interests of Mavera magazine. The occupation and the Afghan resistance were processed with essays, stories and poems, and the developments in the war zone were followed from the ground. In this study, the approaches of the Mavera circle, which Cahit Zarifoğlu evokes with his book Yedi Güzel Adam, will be examined and the subject will be examined by document analysis. The main reference source of the study is the copies of Mavera magazine.
