Makale Başlığı: MOVIES WITH SONG: YESILÇAM
Özet: Yeşilçam, which left its mark on Turkish cinema about 60 years ago, has been imprinted in our memories with its precious directors, star-studded actors and songs. The forerunner sometimes appears as a song that comes out of the language of the protagonist in the movie. It is observed that songs were prepared for films and films were prepared for songs in the period. It is thought that movies with songs in Yeşilçam can attract the attention of the audience even today, when they were shown on the big screen. It can be predicted that the singers who took the leading roles in the movies with songs and the works included, could be a reference to the socio-cultural structure of the period and the right of Turkish music culture. The research exhibits a descriptive approach to due diligence. During the research process, firstly the literature on the subject was scanned, then the opinions of the field experts were taken, and then various comments were made on the subject based on the findings. Afterwards, various results were tried to be reached in the light of findings and comments If some of the research results are listed; In that period, the audience could watch and listen to the artists they admired through films without going to their concerts or casinos, The opportunity to listen to popular works, apart from radio and record labels, by benefiting from the effective power of Turkish cinema in the period, and this opportunity could be the basis for the formation of today's video clips, in Yeşilçam. Some of the things that come to our minds are that even the songs chosen in the movies can give an idea about the movie and its scenario or help in remembering the movie.
