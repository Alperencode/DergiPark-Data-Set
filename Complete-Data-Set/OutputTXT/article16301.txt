Makale Başlığı: Assessing the Role of Work-Life Balance and Flexibility for Employee Well-Being
Özet: The present article is aimed at critically discussing the reviewing the impact of flexibility and work-life balance on employee well-being. Both aspects have been analyzed and with the extensive analysis of the existing theoretical domain. That is to say, the indicators and importance of healthy work-life balance have been discussed. Furthermore, the phenomenon of personal and organization-driven flexibility and its impact on well-being or personnel have also been considered. The causal relationship has been supported between healthy work-life balance and employees’ well-being as well as the role of flexibility in forming and creation of well-being.
