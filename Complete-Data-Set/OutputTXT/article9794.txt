Makale Başlığı: Multi-objective driver scheduling problem in public transportation: a case study in Kırşehir
Özet: The number of passengers traveling in the city is increasing day by day with the rapidly increasing population. The most used transportation method in the city is buses. To ensure the public's satisfaction in urban transportation and to ensure the efficiency of the enterprise to be optimal, it is necessary to make proper pre-planning by the management. In this article, the vehicle and driver scheduling stages of Kırşehir public transportation operations are discussed. Scheduling has been created using the goal programming method to increase operational efficiency and ensure that the person has a fair working environment.
