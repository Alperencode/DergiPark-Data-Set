Makale Başlığı: “Halep” in Classical Turkish Poetry
Özet: Classical Turkish poets sometimes reflect the some  aspects of the cities they have been or traveled to in their  poems. One of this type of cities is Halep which stands  out as an important centre of science, culture and trade.  In this article, which aspects of Halep is reflected in  classical poetry with reference to the poems is tried to be  emphasized.
