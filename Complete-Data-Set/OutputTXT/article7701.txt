Makale Başlığı: Persistence and Academic Performance of Medical Students in Online Learning Environment During the COVID-19 Pandemic Lockdown
Özet: Government response to the COVID 19 pandemic in the spring of 2020 came as a wave of physical closures requiring sudden change in the method of instruction from face-to-face to a completely online. Assessment of student’s adaptation to this change during emergency lockdown is the focus of this study. Students’ test scores related to perception of persistence levels were studied using correlation analysis. In addition, a regression analysis was performed to examine prediction factors of medical student grades during COVID-19.
