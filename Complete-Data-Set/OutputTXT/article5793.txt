Makale Başlığı: INTERNATIONAL FINANCIAL CENTERS: ISTANBUL EVALUATION
Özet: In this study, the ranking and scores of the world's leading financial centers on a global and regional scale were examined within the framework of the Global Financial Center Index. The conditions of being an international financial center are compared with other international financial centers on a global and regional scale, and its current potential has been tried to be revealed. As a result, in order to compete with the world's leading financial centers and to be in the position of an international financial center, it is necessary to ensure economic, social and cultural stability, to create an environment of trust on a global scale and to have a tendency to show continuous improvement.
