Makale Başlığı: Corporate Information and Records Management: Corporate Communication, Records Types, e-Correspondence, Electronic Records Management, Filing Operations
Özet: The 3rd edition of the book "Corporate Information and Document Management", written by Istanbul University Information and Records Management Department Lecturer Prof. Dr. Niyazi ÇİÇEK was published by Marmara Municipalities Union Culture Publications. The purpose of writing the book "Corporate Information and Records Management" is to shed light on the activities related to corporate records management in organizations and to explain the basic concepts related to the field to newly trained candidates. In the book, topics that were known before but not examined from the perspective of the practitioner were discussed.
