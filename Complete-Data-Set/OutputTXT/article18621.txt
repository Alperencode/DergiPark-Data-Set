Makale Başlığı: The Effect Of Motıvatıonal Intervıewıng Method On Self-Effıcacy Levels In Chıldren Wıth Chronıc Dıseases: A Meta-Analysıs Study
Özet: The aim of our study is to investigate the effect of motivational interviewing method on self-efficacy levels in children with chronic disease. For this meta-analysis study, a year-long scan was performed on PubMed, Web of Science, EBSCOhost and Google Academic, Yök thesis databases in January-May 2021 without limitation. After the examinations were made in the study, 5 researches remained and the data were analyzed by meta-analysis method. The total sample size of the studies is 11425. In this meta-analysis, it was found that the motivational interview method in children with chronic diseases had an effect on the self-efficacy level of children (SMD: 0.353, 95% CI: 0.195, 0.510; Z= 4.387, p = 0.000). The measurement tool used in the study, the disease group, the country/continent where the study was conducted, the person who conducted the motivational interview, the chronic disease name moderator were found to be significant (p<0.05). As a result, it was found that the motivational interview method used to support self-efficacy in many chronic diseases also increases the level of self-efficacy in children.
