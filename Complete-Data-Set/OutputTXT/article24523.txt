Makale Başlığı: BİRLEŞİK ZARF-FİİL KAVRAMI VE ÇAĞDAŞ TÜRK LEHÇELERİNDEKİ GÖRÜNÜMLERİ
Özet: Affixes become stereotyped mean; affixes lose their meanings and start to use in different function. Concordantly, status affixes occasionally occur compound structures of gerundium by keeping together with participle, infinitive and tense suffixes. First of all in our study, gerundium and compound gerundium notions will be researched. Subsequent to this, compound gerundiums encountered in Contemporary Turkish Dialects and Literatures will be listed with samples
