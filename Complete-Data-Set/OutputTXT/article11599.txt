Makale Başlığı: A Systematic Review of Purchasing Practices in the Tourism Industry
Özet: Due to the limited research on purchasing and supply management in the tourism industry, which is in the service sector, it has been determined that there is a need for up-to-date and comprehensive studies in recent years. This research is a systematic compilation of studies in the field of purchasing and supply management in the tourism industry. At the same time, it has been determined that purchasing and supply management will provide important research opportunities for the future of academics, practitioners and professional leaders. Using the systematic compilation method, articles were scanned and analyzed in national and international databases according to the determined criteria, including "general scope of production, background theory used, analysis method, research method and main topics researched".However, it has been observed that businesses with a purchasing policy are more prone to open communication, joint problem solving, and have more collaborative and long-term relationships with suppliers. It has been stated that purchasing policies will provide superiority in matters such as competition and supply process, as well as cost advantage to businesses.
