Makale Başlığı: Classes of bi-Starlike and bi-Convex Bounded Functions with Complex Order
Özet: In this paper, estimates for second and third coefficients of certain classes of bi-starlike and bi-convex bounded functions with complex order in the open unit disk are determined, and certain special cases are also indicated.
