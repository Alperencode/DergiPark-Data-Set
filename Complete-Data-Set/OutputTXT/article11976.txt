Makale Başlığı: DETERMINANTS OF HEDGE ACCOUNTING AND IMPACT OF HEDGE ACCOUNTING ON EARNINGS VOLATILITY
Özet: TFRS 9 standard contains regulations on financial hedging and hedge accounting. The aim of this study is to investigate the determinants of hedge accounting application and the effect of hedge accounting on earnings volatility in the BIST Industrial Index. The findings indicate a significant relationship between hedge accounting and firm size, type of independent audit firm and firm age. However, the results do not reveal significant evidence that application of hedge accounting reduces earnings volatility. Study contributes to the limited literature on the subject with its findings.
