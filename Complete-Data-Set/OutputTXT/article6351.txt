Makale Başlığı: FAMİLY AND WOMEN İN THE COMMON CULTURAL HİSTORY OF THE TURKİSH WORLD
Özet: In this work prepared by Abdulkadir Yuvalı and Hikmet Demirci, the family structure and woman are conveyed in detail in Turkish culture. This book is written in a fluent, academic way from the point of view of language and style. In the book, along with archaeological examples, important sources were also used. Such as The Saga of Manas and Divan-ı Lugati't-Turk, which played an important role in Turkish culture. In addition, the chronological transfer of the Turkish family structure from the Asian Hun family structure in the book has provided an understanding of the subject and the observation of how the cultural transfer took place. Also, a detailed inclusion of the traditions in the marriage process provides an important benefit to researchers who want to read about this subject.
