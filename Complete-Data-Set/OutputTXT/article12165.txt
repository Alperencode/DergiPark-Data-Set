Makale Başlığı: Valuation of Design and Samples of Design Valuation Studies
Özet: Designs gain importance day by day due to their financial benefits. This situation increases the importance of design valuation. Thanks to the design valuation, the design, which is an intangible asset, has a monetary value. The monetary values obtained as a result of the valuation provide clarity to the companies in the financial transactions related to the designs. For this reason, design valuation is an area that has gained a lot of importance recently in our country and in the world. In this study, the importance of design valuation, the methods and approaches used in design valuation, and two sample valuation studies were carried out. The data used in the valuation samples prepared using two different methods for the same design were explained and evaluated comparatively.
