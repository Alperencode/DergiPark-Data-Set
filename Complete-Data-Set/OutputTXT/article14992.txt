Makale Başlığı: Qubahan School in Bahdinan in the Reign of Emir  Sultan Hussien Wali (1533- 1573)
Özet: Emirate of Bahdinan (Amedi) is one of the well-known Kurdish emirates, which ruled the Bahdinan region nearly 600 years, a number of scholars and wisdoms emirs appeared in difference stages, they had played key role in scientific, sciences and intellectual fields, they paid attention of building science centers such as religion schools, libraries, available and donating transcripts for teachers and their students. This study is taking movements of sciences and intellectuals into consideration. As Qubahan school under the reign of emir of Bahdinan sultan Hussein wali who ruled between 1533_ 1573. This study is used first hand details, documents and transcripts of that time. This article is being divided into two sections: first section shed lights on the biography of Sultan Hussein Wali from 1533_ 1573 , its second section depicts, Sultan Hussein’s attention for building the science centers.
