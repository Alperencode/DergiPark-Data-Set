Makale Başlığı: MUSIC OF ARGUVAN IN THE CONTEXT OF ACADEMIC LITERATURE
Özet: Keywords: Arguvan, Local Music, Literature. Cultural similarities in ethnic structures, which fed from the same essence in meaning the perception of the world that make up the music, can find specific positions in folk music with their original formal structures in some regions of Anatolian geography. In this perspective, Arguvan creates a field for ethnomusicology studies by making local music attractive with its uniqueness in structure and performance.This study, which aimed to identify and classify the academic literature written about Arguvan region music between the years 1997-2021, is considered important in terms of being a reference for the researches to be made on the music culture of the region and being a reference in the selection of the subject. The research is a descriptive study to determine the situation in terms of the purpose it carries and the quality of the data collected with the method followed in accordance with this purpose. By adopting the scanning model, a total of 31 publications were accessed by literature review, document review and database review such as YÖK National Thesis Center database and Google Scholar; then the related literature was analyzed by content analysis, one of the qualitative data analysis methods, and classified according to their contents.The study includes the findings that the literature on Arguvan region music mainly focuses on religious music, secular music, dialect-dialectic-language features, minstrel
