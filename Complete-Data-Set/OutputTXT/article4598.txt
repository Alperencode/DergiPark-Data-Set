Makale Başlığı: Effects of Pregnant Women’s Fear of the COVID-19 Pandemic on Pregnancy Stress and Traumatic Childbirth Perception
Özet: Objective: The purpose of this study is to determine the effect of pregnant women’s fear of COVID-19 on pregnancy stress and traumatic childbirth perception.Methods: This study utilized a descriptive and relational design and was conducted with a total of 296 pregnant women registered in Family Health Centers located in a city center in eastern Turkey. Data were collected through the Personal Information Form, the Fear of COVID-19 Scale, the Pregnancy Stress Rating Scale, and the Traumatic Childbirth Perception Scale. Data analysis was performed using descriptive statistics (percentage, mean, standard deviation) and t-test in independent groups, analysis of variance, correlation, and regression analysis.Results: The average age of the participating pregnant women was found to be 28.94 ± 5.53.
