Makale Başlığı: MELEE WEAPONS OF THE TUNGUS-MANCHU AND NIVKH  (ON MATERIALS OF MIDDLE XIX–XX CENTURIES)
Özet: In the article the information is presented about arms of hunter, fisher, warrior and the elements of military training. Special attention is paid to the peculiarities of melee weapons of Tungus-Manchu (Evenki , Nanai, Udege, Ulcha, Oroch,. Negidal, Orok) and Nivkh.  Keywords: arms, military arts, traditional culture.
