Makale Başlığı: INTRACRANIAL TUMOR PRESENTING WITH SYMPTOMS OF FRONTAL LOBE SYNDROME
Özet: Frontal lobe syndrome is a disease that can be confused with many neurological, psychiatric diseases and should be kept in mind in differential diagnosis. In this article, I wanted to briefly remind you of frontal lobe syndrome with dementia, delirium symptoms and a case seen in a neurology outpatient clinic.
