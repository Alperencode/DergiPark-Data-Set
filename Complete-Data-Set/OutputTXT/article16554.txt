Makale Başlığı: EVALUATİON OF PRİMARY SCHOOL TEACHERS' ATTİTUDES TOWARDS STUDENTS WİTH SPECİAL LEARNİNG DİFFİCULTİES
Özet: The aim of this research is to determine the attitudes of primary school teachers towards students with special learning dificulties and the factors that influencing these attitudes. The research was conducted with the participation of 130 primary school teachers. Interview technique which is one of the qualitative research methods, was used in the research. In data collection, the "Teacher Information Form" and "Questionnaire Form" were used  which consists of closed-ended questions to obtain demographic information of classroom teachers, prepared by the researchers and taking expert opinion, and the "Interview Form" consisting of open-ended questions were used. As a result of the research, it was determined that the classroom size, teacher proficiency, the support of all concerned in the education process of students with special learning dificulties and the benefit of this education for normal and special learning dificulties students alleviated the workload of the classroom teacher and increased his motivation, at the same time positively affected his attitude. According to the research findings, the types of dificultiy of the students negatively affect the attitudes of the classroom teachers towards the education of students with special learning dificulties. In addition, it has been determined that primary school teachers who have knowledge about the subject have more positive attitudes towards students with special learning dificulties than those who do not. Creating suitable educational environments for the education of students with special learning dificulties and providing support services to classroom teachers that will make them think that they are not alone in this process will increase the success in practice.
