Makale Başlığı: Examınatıon of Classroom Teachers ' Opınıons and Attıtudes on Dıstance Education: A Metasynthesis Study
Özet: In this study, it is aimed to examine the studies on the views and attitudes of classroom teachers about distance education. In this study, metasynthesis method, which is one of the qualitative research types, was used. The obtained data were collected by document analysis technique and analyzed by content analysis. In this research, 12 studies, including four master's theses and eight articles, made in this field about the views and attitudes of classroom teachers about distance education were examined. The data were analyzed within the framework of the themes and general results were tried to be reached and synthesis was made. According to the results of the research, primary school teachers do not have experience with distance education; It has been determined that the problems faced by classroom teachers in the distance education process originate from family, students, teachers and mass media.
