Makale Başlığı: The İmpacts of the Covid-19 Pandemic on the Labor of Working Women in Turkey
Özet: The main purpose of this research is to determine the effects of the Covid-19 pandemic period on the labor of working women in Turkey. This study, which used phenomenology research design, one of the qualitative research designs, was carried out through in-depth interview technique with 10 women from different cities of Turkey. Descriptive analysis method was used in the analysis of the data obtained as the result of the research. In light of the findings obtained from the interviews, it was determined that the paid and unpaid labor processes of women working in Turkey turned into a single shift during the rife, they experienced role conflicts with the increase in paid jobs, domestic labor and caregiver burdens, and unpaid work was mostly carried out by women during the pandemic. The results of the research were compared with previous literature data and the other studies, and necessary suggestions were made for the prevention of social roles based on gender inequality and the solution of the increasing labor problems of women during the rife period.
