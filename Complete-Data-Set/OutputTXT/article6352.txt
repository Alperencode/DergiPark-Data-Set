Makale Başlığı: SİLK ROAD
Özet: The book reviewed is titled "The Silk Road", written by Valerie Hansen in 2012, in which the Silk Road, an important route that shapes the history of the world, is told. The Silk Road was translated into Turkish by İlkem Topal and was published by Nora Kitap in 2021. In this work, which is about one of the most important places not only for commercial exchange but also for intercultural communication, eight cities on the Silk Road route are evaluated by taking their archaeological heritage into the center.
