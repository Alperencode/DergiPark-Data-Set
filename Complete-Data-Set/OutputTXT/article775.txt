Makale Başlığı: An Overview of the Architectural Features of Early Republican School Buildings in Denizli
Özet: With the proclamation of the Republic, institutions with a secular understanding were established to lay the foundations of a modern and civilized society. Reforms were made in this direction throughout the country. Studies in the field of education and training were at the forefront of the new order, created. With the type projects sent to all regions of Anatolia, it is aimed that the entire population is literate and that the modern understanding of education prevails all over the country. In line with the purposes mentioned above, the schools built in Denizli during the Early Republican Period have survived to the present day. These schools, built in the Early Republican Period, contain the characteristics of the I. National Architectural style. When we look at the general characteristics of the historical schools in Denizli, most of them are placed in a rectangular area, and the masses at both ends of the short sides are projected to form an I plan scheme. In addition, a dynamic appearance has been given to the facades with monumental entrance axes and arched window arrangements. The simple but dynamic appearance of the building is supported by the hipped roof system and wide eaves. The historical schools, built in Denizli in the Early Republican Period, in which male and female students study together and co-education is dominant, appear as functional structures.
