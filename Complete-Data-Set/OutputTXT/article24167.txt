Makale Başlığı: The relationship between contextual factors and internship experience
Özet: In this research, the relationship between internship-related contextual factors (flexible working hours, pay, commute and desirable firm location) and the internship experience of university students was investigated. Within the scope of the research, 200 university students from different departments in Turkey replied to a questionnaire. As a result, a significant relationship of commute and location of company with the internship experience was found. Furthermore,flexible working hours was found to have a significant relationship with perceived learning. In addition, dissatisfaction, discomfort and internship experience differ significantly between paid interns and non-paid interns. The contribution of the article findings to the internship process and various future research avenues is discussed.
