Makale Başlığı: The 5th e-BEYAS Symposium will be held in Ankara between 10-11 October 2022
Özet: e-BEYAS 2022 Symposium hosted by Ankara University, organized by Ankara University Information Management Systems Certification and Information Security Center (BİL-BEM), Ankara University Records Management and Archive System (BEYAS) Coordinator, Ankara University Information Processing Department and Information Management Journal. It will be held in Ankara between 10-11 October 2022 with the contribution and support of TÜRKSAT.
