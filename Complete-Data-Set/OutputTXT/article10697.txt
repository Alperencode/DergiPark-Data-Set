Makale Başlığı: Pseudo Simplicial Algebras, Crossed Modules and 2-Crossed Modules
Özet: In this paper we define pseudo 2-crossed module of commutativealgebras and we give relations between the pseudo 2-crossed modulesof commutative algebras and pseudo simplicial algebras with Moorecomplex of length 2.
