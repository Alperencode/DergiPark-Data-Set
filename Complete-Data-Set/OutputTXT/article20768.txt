Makale Başlığı: FEELING OF CONFIDENCE IN TIMES OF CRISIS: THE EXAMPLE OF THE COVID-19 PANDEMIC
Özet: The purpose of this study, Covidien-19 pandemics in the process of individual state institutions in Turkey, the economy and Covidien-19 education with confidence for the vaccine, professional and Covidien-19 to investigate the relationship between the conspiracy assessment for the pandemic. The study was conducted within the framework of quantitative research; Accordingly, a questionnaire titled "Covid19 Pandemic and Trust" was created and applied online to the participants. The data of the study, which was carried out with the participation of 294 people, was analyzed with the SPSS20 program. As a result, it was found that there is a significant relationship between trust in state institutions and education level and profession, participants have the highest level of trust in healthcare workers compared to other options; It has been stated that there is a significant relationship between the thought that the covid-19 virus is a conspiracy and the vaccine trust. Progressive era in the research will be to examine more comprehensive sense of confidence in the political and social institutions in Turkey and the inclusion of the research potential of the variables affecting the confidence in these institutions has been expressed can provide more comprehensive results. On the other hand, it was stated that comparing the data obtained from similar studies at the end of the covid-19 pandemic and the researches on the sense of trust during the covid-19 pandemic process will also contribute to making inferences about the variables that affect the sense of trust.
