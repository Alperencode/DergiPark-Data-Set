Makale Başlığı: A Study On The “Time Regulation Institute” on The Axis Of Social Structure, Religion And Change
Özet: All kinds of artistic, literary and scientific products and activities, which are obtained in terms of reflecting the change experiences of a society, can turn into very important resources for those interested in the subject, which they can interpret by concentrating on different angles. In this respect, literary works of novel type also provide very important literature and archive information in terms of monitoring and examining the change processes that Turkish society has experienced. In this article, from the perspective of Ahmet Hamdi Tanpınar, who has closely witnessed the modernization efforts of Turkish society, which is also defined as attempts to change civilization, the effects and results of the current dynamics of change on social structure elements will be tried to be discussed under various titles by centered on the the novel " Time Regulation Institute". The questions to be answered in line with various comparisons and evaluations were formed as follows: How are institutions such as family, education, religion, politics, that make up society in sociological terms, handled and evaluated in the rhetoric of this novel? How is the functioning cycle of these institutions in the social structure given? How are the contexts in which religiosity is addressed within this scale of change? What are the effects and consequences of the current change on these institutional structures? In the research, it will be tried to make maximum use of the qualitative method's document analysis, literature review and comparison techniques.
