Makale Başlığı: Some Identities for Rearick's Hyperbolic Trigonometric Functions
Özet: In this article, we consider some properties provided by Rearick's hyperbolic trigonometric functions. We arrange and present some of the properties provided by Rearick's hyperbolic trigonometric functions, taking into account the properties of the basic hyperbolic trigonometric functions.
