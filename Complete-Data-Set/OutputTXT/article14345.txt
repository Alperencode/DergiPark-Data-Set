Makale Başlığı: CONCEPTUALIZING ENERGY EFFICIENCY WITHIN THE SCOPE OF ENERGY SECURITY AND ENERGY SUSTAINABILITY
Özet: As energy is essential to life utilizing energy resources efficiently has been at the top of the agendas of the governments and energy efficiency has been among the major components of energy policies so far. However it is not that easy to achieve energy efficiency as it is inherently related to the other components of sustainability. Sustainability plays a key role in development goals of the countries and refers to sustainable economic, social, environmental, political and security policies. It is critical to promote sustainability showing regard to each of them without any conflict. This research presents a comprehensive understanding of energy efficiency focusing on energy sustainability and energy security. It is aimed to better understand energy efficiency within the scope of energy sustainability and energy security.
