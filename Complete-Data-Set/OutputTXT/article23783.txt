Makale Başlığı: Cultural Problems Of Afghan Youth From Multicultural Social Work Perspective: The Case Of Usak Province
Özet: This study aims to determine the problems of Afghan youth at the cultural crossing point and to examine the findings in depth from the perspective of multicultural social work. It is thought that the results to be obtained from the study will reveal the problems of immigrants at the point of cultural crossing, in particular Afghans, and will contribute to both the field experts and the literature on the solution to these problems. The study was carried out within the framework of qualitative research methodology and in-depth individual interviews were conducted with Afghan youth residing in Usak. The findings reached within the framework of the study were analyzed by content analysis descriptively and interpreted by us. As a result of the findings obtained; It has been determined that the family structure of Afghan youth has undergone a great change in the post-migration period, the extended family structure they have in their country of origin has turned into a nuclear family after the migration, and the status of women in family life has increased. In the context of working life, religious life, and social and cultural life, it is seen that Afghan youth have experienced an important process of change and transformation. The study has importance in terms of evaluating these experienced cultural crossing processes from the perspective of multicultural social work.
