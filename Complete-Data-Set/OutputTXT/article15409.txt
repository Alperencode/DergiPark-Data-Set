Makale Başlığı: Notes on Cryptodrassus creticus (Araneae: Gnaphosidae) from Turkey
Özet: The species of Cryptodrassus creticus Chatzaki, 2002 is only known from female specimen in Turkey. In this study, we found male specimens of this species for the first time and add new locality to its distribution in Anatolia. Its general habitus and genitalia are illustrated.
