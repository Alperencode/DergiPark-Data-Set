Makale Başlığı: Evaluation of ‘Hedwig’ in Hedwig and the Angry Inch as the Jester
Özet: This study examines the character of ‘Hedwig’ in the musical play Hedwig and the Angry  Inch,  written  by  John  Cameron  Mitchell,  using  the  historical  concept  and  typology of the jester, with a special focus on performers’ perspectives. First, the study focuses on the historical origin of the concept of jester and its development over  various  historical  periods.  Afterward,  the  character  of  Hedwig  is  analysed  from a historical perspective to determine which kinds of jester concept Hedwigmight correspond to today. Evaluating the position of the character and her lines in  the  play  was  significant  in  this  determination.  The  study  concludes  with  an  assessment of the qualitative evaluation method. The findings of this examination will present a new perspective to researchers and actors to evaluate the character of Hedwig through the concept and typology of the jester.
