Makale Başlığı: NUMERICAL SOLUTION OF AN INVERSE PROBLEM FOR THE LIOUVILLE EQUATION
Özet: We consider an inverse problem for the Liouville Equation. We present the solvability conditions and obtain numerical solution of the problem based on the nite dierence approximation.
