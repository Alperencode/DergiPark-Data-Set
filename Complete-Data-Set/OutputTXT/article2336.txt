Makale Başlığı: Domestication and Origins of the Modern Horse Breeds
Özet: This study aimed to collect some studies which focused on modern horse breeds' genetic origins and effects of domestication on horses. Domestication, the first step of using animals by humanity. Many variations are shaped modern animal species and breeds, also animal breeding techniques and science. Horses have a place in livestock animals from the past to the present day. Many studies focused on the genetic origins and domestication of modern horse breeds. Recent studies demonstrated domestication areas, and genetic origins of horses, and changed in filial trees.
