Makale Başlığı: Mirza Melkum Khan and His Role in the Ottoman Tanzimat
Özet: In the 19th century, Iranian politician and thinker Mirza Melkum Khan Nazımüd-devle lived in Istanbul for more than ten years during the Tanzimat period of the Ottoman Empire. He had relations with Fuad Pasha, Ali Pasha, and Midhat Pasha, who were among the most important figures of the First Constitutional Monarchy and Tanzimat era. Melkum Khan later claimed that he wrote the will of FuadPasha and the empire benefited from this text for the First Constitutional Law of the Ottoman Empire.He also claimed that he wrote Ali Pasha's will. This article will analyze and characterize Melkum Khan'sworks and documents, look at his main historical sources and explain the accuracy of Melkum Khan'sclaims.
