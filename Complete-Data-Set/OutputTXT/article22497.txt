Makale Başlığı: Foundation Pathology in Society
Özet: Foundational economy is one of the less studied topics by researchers, despite its vast capacity in the application of scientific and social issues. Apart from the socio-economic dimension of fiqh, no comprehensive scientific research has been conducted in other dimensions. It should also be said that the foundation culture has not been fully institutionalized due to some problems in Islamic societies. In this article, it has been tried to examine the problems and obstacles in front of the development of donation culture in society. In the article, after examining various aspects that damag the foundation culture, general and scientific solutions are expressed to improve the donation function in the society. Finally, suggestions and inferences were made about donation and foundation culture.
