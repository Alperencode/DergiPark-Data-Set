Makale Başlığı: On Some Classes of Generalized Recurrent  α-Cosymplectic Manifolds
Özet: In this paper, we concentrate on hyper generalized $\varphi-$recurrent $\alpha-$cosymplectic manifolds and quasi generalized $\varphi-$recurrent $\alpha-$cosymplectic manifolds and obtain some significant characterizations which classify such manifolds.
