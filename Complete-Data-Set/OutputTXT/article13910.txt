Makale Başlığı: Political Structure of Denizli and Its Vicinity During the Period of the Second Crusade
Özet: The Germans and French the lead of second Crusade. The Germans are near Eskişehir suffered a heavy defeat and returned to Iznik. French forces started to advance towards Denizli, they were exposed intensive Turkmen attacks. To meet their food needs, they aimed to visit the city of Laodikeia near Denizli. However, the Greek people of this city had left Louis could not find what he had hoped in Laodikeia and intended to cross Honaz Mountain as soon as possible and reach safer regions. On January 7, 1148, he decided to cross this highest mountain in the region of Aegean. However, the Turkmens who know the region well they dealt a great blow to the Crusaders with the ambush they prepared. One of the biggest reason for this defeat is Turkmen density in Denizli and around was not noticed by the Crusaders.As this way is safe was considered. Although the region seems under Byzantine control, Turkmens had full control over Denizli and its surroundings. The sovereignty of Byzantium was for show. Crusaders who could not realize this, took a heavy defeat in Denizli.
