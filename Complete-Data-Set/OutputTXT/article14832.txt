Makale Başlığı: A Review of Key Concepts in Language Maintenance: Directions for Maintaining Endangered Languages in Turkey
Özet: The aim of this paper is to review some key concepts in language maintenance, project on some examples of language maintenance efforts from around the world, and offer directions for taking precautions against the death of endangered languages in Turkey. First, the paper draws attention to the problem of endangerment of languages. Based on UNESCO accounts, it discusses the endangered languages in Turkey. By referring to existing literature on language maintenance, it introduces the key concepts that play part in language vitality. Later, it offers some examples of language maintenance efforts from around the world. In the conclusion, it suggests ways to maintain endangered languages in Turkey by drawing on the factors involved in language vitality.
