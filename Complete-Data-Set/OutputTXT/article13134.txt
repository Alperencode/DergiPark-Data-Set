Makale Başlığı: Use Of Unpaid Chıld Labor In Rural Lıfe
Özet: This study was designed to reveal the ways in which child labor is used in the rural area of Van. The main claim of the research is that any kind of work is a harmful process for children. Although there are many studies on child labor, there is a great lack of studies on unpaid child labor, which is widely used in the home, in the literature. In this respect, it is aimed to draw a picture of unpaid child labor over the province of Van. 741 questionnaires were conducted with children working at home in rural areas. The data obtained; It has been analyzed under the main headings of "socio-demographic and economic characteristics", "working conditions and risks", "school experiences and future expectations". As a result of the study, it was seen that; Working space and conditions have negative effects for children who work intensively in domestic work in rural areas. The fact that children work in physically and psychologically challenging jobs negatively affects both their school and future expectations. Most of the families of children working in rural areas are low-income and low-education families. The low social and economic level of families is an extremely important factor in (employing) children.
