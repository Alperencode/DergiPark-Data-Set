Makale Başlığı: GEOGRAPHY EDUCATION IN ENGLAND
Özet: In order for geography teaching to reach the ideal level, it is of great importance to examine international practices. In this study, which examines geography education in England from different perspectives, geography teaching in England was examined in terms of the English geography curriculum, physical environment, materials used in the lessons, textbooks, teacher training, methods used and problems in geography education. The research is a qualitative research and the data were obtained by document analysis method and interpreted in a descriptive manner. As a result of the research, Royal Geographical Society is effective in geography education in England, 1st, 2nd, 3rd grade schools in schools. It has been determined that there are some problems in geography education, such as the fact that the classes are taught for 2 hours a week, the physical environments are rich in terms of lessons, the textbooks are not adhered to, the teachers are graduated from three-year departments, the work in groups is predominant in the lessons, and the teachers have to develop resources before the lesson. In addition, it has been determined that schools are rich in materials used in lessons, and schools and universities give importance to field trips.
