Makale Başlığı: Democratic Tendencies of Teacher Candidates on the Basis of Social Studies Education
Özet: Individuals need some knowledge, values, attitudes, and skills to live together in decency and adapt to society. The most important of these skills is democratic skills. Democratic skills that ensure the socialization of the individual and continuation of society should be systematically taught in schools by teachers with democratic skills. Education of individuals adopting democratic values is possible within the school environment, only together with teachers who adopt these values. There is a limited number of studies examining the democratic tendencies of teacher candidates. In this direction, this study aims to determine the democratic tendencies of teacher candidates on the basis of Social Studies Teachers. In this study, the 5-likert type “Teacher Candidate Democratic Tendency Scale” developed by Akbaşlı et al. (2010) was used to collect data. Preschool teaching, Turkish teaching, and social studies teacher candidates at Ege University and Dokuz Eylül University during September 2021-2022 Spring semester were selected as the study group and the scale was applied to 423 people. The data were analyzed with SPSS 21 program. As a result, the democratic tendencies of these teacher candidates were reached and the relationship between these deciencies and the candidates of teachers in 4 different branches was revealed.
