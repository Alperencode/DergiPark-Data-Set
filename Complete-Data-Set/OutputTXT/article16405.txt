Makale Başlığı: ANNIHILATION OF STATISM  IN THE PROCESS OF ITS ARTICULATION TO WORD CAPITALISM IN THE TURKISH REPUBLIC
Özet: This study claims to be a historical and analytical study about the development of capitalism in Turkey. This study, it is aimed to determine how the transformation in Turkey has occurred in the context of the economical and political dynamics with the transition into the world capitalist system took place in terms of domestic and international conditions, and ultimately to reveal how and in what way this process affected the social classes in Turkey.In this context, the policies and results of the governments established in the economic, political, and social living conditions in Turkey in the Second World War environment were evaluated; and then the implementation results of these policies were tried to be examined in the context of the country's efforts to articulate with the world capitalism. In this context, after mentioning some domestic laws and the foreign political developments affecting the process of articulation with capitalism; the developments before and after the political decisions of September 7, 1946, Turkey's first major exchange rate adjustment, are focused on. In the last phase of the process of articulation into capitalism, emphasis was placed on the importance of the Truman Doctrine, Marshall Plan, and reports from the USA as the external developments that accelerated the aforementioned process; as well as the effect of the Americanist current in the Turkish Grand National Assembly, governments and intellectual circles in the context of internal developments to the ideological transformation of statism and CHP and finally mentioning the crucial value of the 1948 Turkish Economy Congress, which was one of the last stops on the way to the "Free World".
