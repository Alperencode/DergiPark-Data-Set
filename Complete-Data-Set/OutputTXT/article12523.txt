Makale Başlığı: Pre-school Educational Institutions on E-Complaints Sites: "I Have a Complaint!"
Özet: The aim of this study was to investigate the complaints written to the e-complaint sites for pre-school education institutions in Turkey. For this purpose, analytical research model was used, and document analysis was used in data analysis. Within the scope of the research, complaints were searched using the keywords "kindergarten", "kindergarten" and "preschool" on the website "Şikayet Var" and each complaint was recorded. As a result of the screening, 377 complaints were reached. The " Complaint Determination Document Analysis Form" created by the researcher was used as the data collection tool. A total of 7 themes were formed because of the research. These are financial reasons, child harm, teacher-related reasons, administrative reasons, registration process, recruitment process and physical facilities. The most common type of complaint is financial reasons. In addition, it was determined that the complaints were mostly made for private pre-school education institutions and that complaints were mostly written by women.
