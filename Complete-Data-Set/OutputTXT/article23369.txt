Makale Başlığı: Abdurrahman Kasapoğlu, The Quran Culture of Atatürk
Özet: In this study, the book, The Quran Culture of Atatürk by Apdurrahman Kasapoğlu is introduced. In the book, information about the Quran is included. Moreover, The influence of the Qur'an in every period of Atatürk's life is considered periodically. In the work, especially Atatürk's speeches, letters and the views of the managers and thinkers of the period on the subject are included.
