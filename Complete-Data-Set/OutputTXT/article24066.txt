Makale Başlığı: 2006 ASSESSMENT OF UZBEKISTAN'S COHESION AND POLITICAL INDICATORS IN THE VULNERABLE COUNTRIES INDEX
Özet: The Fragile Countries Index has been evaluating countries every year since 2006 in four categories: compatibility, political, economic and social. This study is limited to compliance and policy indicators only. Uzbekistan ranked 22nd in this list in 2006. The issues that underlie its high scores on compliance and policy indicators are discussed.
