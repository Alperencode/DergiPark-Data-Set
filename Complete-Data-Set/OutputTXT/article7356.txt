Makale Başlığı: m-quasi Einstein Metric and Paracontact Geometry
Özet: The object of the upcoming article is to characterize paracontact metric manifolds conceding $m$-quasi Einstein metric. First we establish that if the metric $g$ in a $K$-paracontact manifold is the $m$-quasi Einstein metric, then the manifold is of constant scalar curvature. Furthermore, we classify $(k,\mu)$-paracontact metric manifolds whose metric is $m$-quasi Einstein metric. Finally, we construct a non-trivial example of such a manifold.
