Makale Başlığı: A Case of Smith-Magenis Syndrome
Özet: Smith-Magenis syndrome is a genetic disorder characterized by facial dysmorphism, developmental delay, cognitive   retardation, characteristic behavior pattern and sleep disturbance. In this study we report a Smith Magenis Syndrome patient   with facial dysmorphism, developmental delay and cognitive retardation.
