Makale Başlığı: The Scale of the Social Assistance Providers' Perception of Social Assistance: Validity and Reliability Study
Özet: The scale of perception of social assistance of those who give social assistance is a scale created as a result of fieldwork in the field. As a result of qualitative interviews with SYDV employees in various provinces and districts in Turkey, the perceptions of social assistance providers towards the social assistance system were tried to be determined. The main purpose of the research is to conduct the validity and reliability study of the scale obtained from the data collected through qualitative interviews. The research was carried out with 358 people obtained by stratified sampling method in Turkey. As a result of the study, a validity and reliability study were developed, with 5 factors and 17 items with a total variance of 58.54%. In this context, the sub-dimensions of the scale were formed as "Social aid recipients", "functioning in social aids", "institutional capacity", "access to public services" and "social aid and social service". The model fit of the scale structure formed by exploratory factor analysis was tested with confirmatory factor analysis
