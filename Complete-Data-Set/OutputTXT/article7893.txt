Makale Başlığı: Imigration from Romania to Turkey (1934-1939)
Özet: The Turkish Republic has aproached immigrants and the imigration problem as a big national matter. The government has put big efforts to make immigrants beneficial individuals. According to our research the migrations happened between the years 1934-1939 have been optional and government supported. Following Turkey’s development and economical, Turkey has encouraged the migrations and the Romanian government have been on board with this policy. The procceses for migrations are as followed: the instructions given to the ambassador, housing of the immigrants, the creation of inmigrant villages, the providing of farming equipments, providing farming animals, land distribution, educating the immigrants to become producers. The steamers carrying the immigrants were firstly brought to the storehouses, here they go trough sanitary processes, they are hosted in guesthouses and finally are send to their respectfull settlement areas. The immigrants and the migration problem were all dealt with great delicacy because it was featured constantly in the newspapers. This research aims to evaluate the conditions of the immigrants migrating from Romania to Turkey and the environment of the immigrants from 1934 to 1939, the problems immigrants faced and the resources they with the help of newspapers and archives of the time being.
