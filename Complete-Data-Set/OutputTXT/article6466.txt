Makale Başlığı: Reflections of Mythological Elements Used by Phrygians on Regional Tourism
Özet: In this study, the projections of the cultural elements of the Phrygian in tourism perspective were examined within the scope of Afyon, Kütahya and Eskişehir. While determining the projections, traditional mythologies were used. For this purpose, an analysis was carried out on the web pages prepared for Phrygian Region promotion. The use of Phrygian cultural elements for touristic purposes in this region was evaluated. The data of the study were collected from Afyon, Kütahya and Eskişehir Municipality and Governorship web pages (49) and were recorded manually in the "Scan Registration Form". Survey was conducted between December 2021 and February 2022. As a result, it was determined that some mythological elements showed similar characteristics. These expressions were combined and seven categories (places to visit, touristic businesses, museums, economy, gastronomy, local products, art and festivals) were determined. Research results; It shows that the traces of Phrygians prevail in the naming of the places to visit in these three cities, the theme of their museums, their gastronomy, the names of saloons in touristic establishments, local products, economic structure, arts and festivals.
