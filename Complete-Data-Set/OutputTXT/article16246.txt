Makale Başlığı: AN APPLIED STUDY ON THE PROBLEMS THAT PRE-SCHOOL TEACHERS EXPERIENCED FACE-TO-FACE EDUCATION DURING THE PANDEMIC PROCESS
Özet: The Covid-19 epidemic has led to the shaping and transformation of educational activities, as in all areas of social life. Many studies on the subject have been put forward nationally and internationally. Since distance education has such a widespread use for the first time, the risks have also developed together. In this process, pre-school education, which requires face-to-face education together with distance education, is included in a special field. In this study, despite the transition to the distance education system in education-teaching activities during the Covid-19 process, it focuses on the face-to-face pre-school education. Many problems were experienced in this process, where teachers experienced serious uneasiness for both children and themselves and their families. Children are not controllable to follow the mask and distance rules as they are young. For this reason, although necessary, many very risky situations have become inevitable. In this context, a study was conducted with 24 preschool teachers on this process and its effects. In order to ensure the diversity and efficiency of the study, teachers; It has been selected by focusing on differences according to age, gender, marital status, city of residence and professional seniority. In the study, using the qualitative research method, the data were provided in writing, by transmitting notes or by voice recording, and an ethics committee document was obtained for the interview questions.
