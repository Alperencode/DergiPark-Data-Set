Makale Başlığı: Kabuğunu Kıran Hikâye: An Outlook on Turkish Storytelling in the 1950 Generation
Özet: '' Kabuğunu Kıran Hikaye '' is redacted and reviewed form of Jale Özata Dirlikyapan's doctoral thesis.  In this doctoral thesis, Dirlikyapan discusses the story writers who brought novelty and started their writing life in 1950-1960 which was a period of successive political and social alteration in Turkey, and also occuring a new urban form as a result of rural-urban migration.
