Makale Başlığı: Mentioning Animals in the Qur'an, Their Wisdom and Benefits
Özet: In the Qur'an, Allah mentions that He created many worlds. One of these realms is the animal kingdom. Allah has stated that animals are given to humans as an important blessing, that humans benefit from animals in various ways, that animals are an ornament of the worldly life, and that humans should take a lesson from animals.Mankind has benefited from animals in many ways since the day it was created. Animals have been an important part of human life. In the Qur'an, animals appear as proof of the existence of Allah, prophethood and resurrection. When these verses about animals are examined, the existence and unity of Allah and the wisdom behind the creation of animals will be better understood. It is extremely important for people to understand the purpose and wisdom in the creation of animals, which have many benefits for them, and to obey their rights, as well as being an expression of gratitude, in terms of preserving the ecological balance in the world.The aim of this study is to identify the animals mentioned in the Qur'an and to reveal the benefits they provide. As a result, people should better understand the purpose and wisdom of the creation of animals and respect their rights.
