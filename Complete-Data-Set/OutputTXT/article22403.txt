Makale Başlığı: Screen Semi-invariant Lightlike Submanifolds of a Golden Semi-Riemannian Manifold
Özet: In this paper, we study the geometry of screen semi-invariant lightlike submanifolds of a golden semi-Riemannian manifold. The intergrability conditions of distributions S(TN) and RadTN on screen semi-invariant lightlike submanifolds of a golden semi-Riemannian manifold are obtained. Further, we derive necessary and su_cient conditions for above distributions to be totally geodesic foliations.
