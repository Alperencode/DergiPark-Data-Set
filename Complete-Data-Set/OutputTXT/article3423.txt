Makale Başlığı: Stability of Solutions for a Krichhoff-Type Plate Equation with Degenerate Damping
Özet: We investigate a Kirchhoff type plate equation with degenerate damping term. By potential well theory, we show the asymptotic stability of energy in the presence of a degenerate damping.
