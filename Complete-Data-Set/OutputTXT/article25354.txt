Makale Başlığı: On the decomposition of curvature tensor fields in conformal Finsler spaces II
Özet: The object of the present paper is to decompose curvature tensor fields in conformal Finsler spaces. The authors have also studied properties of the conformal decomposition tensor fields. The notations used in the sequel are due to H.Rund [4].
