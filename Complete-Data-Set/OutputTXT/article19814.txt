Makale Başlığı: REVIEW OF THE ORAL HISTORY STUDY "KARADENIZ EREGLI WITH BLACK-AND-WHITE PHOTOGRAPHS" WITHIN THE SCOPE OF LOCAL HISTORY AND CULTURE (OPTIONAL) COURSE
Özet: Since the 2013-2014 School Term, the book: “ Oral History Study of Karadeniz Ereğli with Black-and-White Photographs” is forming the content of “Local History and Culture Lesson” which is given as an elective course to 3rd Class students of Elementary Section, Social Studies Education Department. In this study, it has been aimed to create an example to researchers and to support the usage of local history and oral history studies in lessons and education of concept, value and skill which are in the Education Program of Social Studies.
