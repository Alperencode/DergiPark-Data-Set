Makale Başlığı: Construction of Collective Memory Through Narrative: Jerrahis
Özet: This study aims to explain the construction of the collective memory of dervishes through narrative with the example of an active Jerrahi lodge in Istanbul. In this research, the rituals of the tariqat are examined in depth and how social memory is built through speeches, stories and hymns in the lodge is explained. Qualitative method was applied in the field research, and data were collected through "observation by participation" and "interview" in this process. Between August 2019 and March 2020, the researcher regularly attended the meşks and dhikrs held twice a week at the Jerrahi lodge in Istanbul. Semi-structured interviews were conducted with 10 different dervishes to deepen the data obtained during the observation process by participating. This study is a presentation of the data obtained during the ethnographic field research process, and that it is the first study conducted on a Jerrahi lodge other than the Karagümrük Jerrahi Lodge. Within the scope of the research, “How is social memory constructed in the Jerrahis through narrative?” question addressed, and it was found that the collective memory of the community members was built with the constant flow of culture.
