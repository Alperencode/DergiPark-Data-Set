Makale Başlığı: SECOND WORLD WAR DURING STARTING, TURKEY'S FOREIGN POLICY (1939)
Özet: The leading countries of the world in 1939 rapidly prepared for a new world war, small countries thatthe Turkey also among this, showed their utmost diplomatic efforts to keep themselves away from thewar. In this context, The year 1939 can be considered as a year in which important political andmilitary developments in terms of world history were formed, and even the fate of the twentiethcentury took shape. Turkey, negotiated with Britain and France throughout a significant part of 1939to form an alliance against the threat by the Axis block and at the end of this period The Turkish -British - French Alliance Treaty was signed in October 1939. With This alliance, Turkey has opted totake place among the Western democracies to replace the old ally Germany.Throughout 1939, although Turkey and its Western allies make efforts to have entered into an alliancewith the Soviet Union these efforts were not successful. The Soviet Union preferred cooperation withGermany. 1939 also, continuing since the period of the National Struggle the good relations betweenthe Soviet Union and Turkey has rapidly deteriorated , the two sides prefered to get on different andrival block side.
