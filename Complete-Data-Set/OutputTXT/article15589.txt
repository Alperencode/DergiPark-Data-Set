Makale Başlığı: The Properties of Projective, Concircular and Conharmonic Curvature Tensor Fields on a Complex Sasakian Manifold
Özet: In this article, the properties of projective, concircular and conharmonic curvature tensor fields on a complex Sasakian manifold are investigated.
