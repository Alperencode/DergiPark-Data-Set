Makale Başlığı: Is Dermal Melasma a Phototoxic Reaction?: A Case Report
Özet: Pigmentationrelated to drugs consist %10-20 of cases with acquired hyperpigmentation. Melasmais a common acquired disorder characterized by symmetric, hyperpigmentedpatches with an irregular outline, occurring most commonly on the face. Melasmais rarely seen in males. High ultraviolet exposure and family history are themost common in ethiology  and some drugsmay result melasma to be seen in males which is very rare. Phototoxic drugreaction should also be considered in differential diagnosis in male patientswith hyperpigmentation on the sun exposed areas.
