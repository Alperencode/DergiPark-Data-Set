Makale Başlığı: MEMDUH ŞEVKET ESENDAL’IN YİRMİ KURUŞ ADLI HİKYESİNİN GÖSTERGEBİLİMSEL BİR ANALİZİ
Özet: Mahmut Şevket Esendal,writer in republic period and especially displayironic and tragic stuations of people of town and vintage in his stories that he wrote, depict that Halil İbrahim who is young and poor peasant train to cash twenty kuruş from Salim Ağa and his neighbourhood. When Halil İbrahim who is main character of this story train to cash, he come up ironic and tragic situations; and finally to success be come impossible. His poorness, Salim Ağa and his neighbourhood’s richness go on. In this study, it will be analized this story and will be reveal edits levels with datas of semeiologic criticsm that Greimas, Barthes and Todorov formed for literary criticism
