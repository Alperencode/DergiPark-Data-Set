Makale Başlığı: The flora of Dutlu Mountain (Oltu, Erzurum) and its surroundings
Özet: In this study, the flora of Dutlu dağ and its surroundings located in the southeast of the Eastern Black Sea Region, between 2019-2022 was investigated. In this study, a total of 768 taxa belonging to 86 families and 394 genera were determined. All of these taxa belong to the Tracheophyta division. 6 of the taxa belong to Polypodiopsida, 5 of them Pinopsida, 1 of them Gnetopsida, 679 of them Magnoliopsida, 77 of them belong to Liliopsida. The families with the highest number of taxa in the research area; Asteraceae 122, Fabaceae 64, Lamiaceae 61, Rosaceae 50, Brassicaceae 42, Caryophyllaceae 37, Poaceae 31, Apiaceae 30 and Boraginaceae 26. The floristic regions of 331 taxa (43.10%) determined in the study area were determined. The distribution of taxa according to phytogeographical regions is as follows: 163 (21.20%) Iran-Turanian, 95 (12.40%) are Europe-Siberia, 26 (3.40%) Mediterranean origin, 47 (6.10%) cosmopolitan. 82 endemic plants have been identified in the research area, and the endemism rate is 10.67%. Endemic and non-endemic plants are classified according to IUCN hazard categories.
