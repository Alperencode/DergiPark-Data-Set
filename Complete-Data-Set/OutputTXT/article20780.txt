Makale Başlığı: The Age of Deception : Nuclear Diplomacy in Treacherous Times
Özet: This study is the review of the book  "The Age of Deception : Nuclear Diplomacy in Treacherous Times" (2016), which includes especially in Middle East experiences of nuclear diplomacy of  Mohamed ElBaradei, who was the Director General of the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) between 1997 and 2009.
