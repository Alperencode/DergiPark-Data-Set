Makale Başlığı: Volar Dislocation of the Metacarpophalangeal Joint of the Thumb: A Case Report
Özet: A patient with a volar dislocation of the metacarpophalangeal joint of the thumb is presented. Volar dislocation of the metacarpophalangeal joint of the thumb is a really rare condition. Open reduction was applied through a dorsal incision. Because of the soft tissue interposition e.g; dorsal capsule, volar plate, dislocated extensor pollicis longus and brevis tendons, ruptured ulnar collateral ligament; open reduction, soft tissue and ligament repair are recommended for this type of thumb dislocations. DOI: 10.3944/AOTT.2015.15.0104This abstract belongs to the un-edited version of the article and is only for informative purposes.
