Makale Başlığı: THE RELATION BETWEEN RELIGION AND CIVILIZATION IN THE UNDERSTANDING OF YUSUF YAKUBOV WHO İS THE LAST PERIOD BULGARIAN SCHOLAR
Özet: Hâfız Yusuf Yakubov (Şinasi) is one of the people from Bulgaria who had significant influence and service on the religious life of Bulgarian Muslims in the vital period after leaving the Ottoman Empire. In 1898, Hafiz Yusuf was born in Işıkköy (Osenovets) which is district of Shumen. He became one of the successful students and outstanding teachers in Medresetü'n-Nüvvâb.  Hâfız Yusuf started his publishing career during his student years and worked as a writer and editor in journals such as İntibah, Sada-yı İslam, and Medeniyet, which had a considerable impact on the thought of Bulgarian Muslims.  After his graduation, he worked as a teacher at Nüvvâb, apart from his duties as a member of the Chief Mufti's Divan-ı Âli-i Şer'i (High Council of Judiciary) and the general secretary of the "Community of Advocates for the Islam Religion".  Hâfız Yusuf is reputed to immigrate to Turkey in 1950 and died in Eskişehir in 1952.  In this study was given information about the life and personality of Hâfız Yusuf in general and the series of articles published by him in the Medeniyet newspaper under the heading "The Essence of Civilization is Religion" were transliterated and the view of civilization was analyzed based on these articles.
