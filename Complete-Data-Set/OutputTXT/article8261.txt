Makale Başlığı: THE VIEWS OF MUSIC TEACHERS ABOUT ACCOMPANIMENT OF SONGS WITH MAQAM CONTENT IN SECONDARY SCHOOLS MUSIC TEXTBOOKS
Özet: In thıs research, it is aimed to take and evaluate the opinions of music teachers about accompaniment of songs with maqam content in secondary school music textbooks. School songs with maqam content in music textbooks of the 5th, 6th, 7th and 8th grades of secondary school were examined in terms of maqam, cadence sounds and tempos, and they were classified by the grades. In the research, a causal comparison method was used in order to scan for the determination of current situations and to determine the thoughts of music teachers about their self-efficacy according to graduation types. Within the scope of the research, the first part of the questionnaire applied to music teachers; questions about personal information, and the second part consists of 34 survey questions to determine the status of being able to accompany songs with modal content. A questionnaire was applied to music teachers working in secondary schools in order to obtain quantitative data on accompaniment of songs with modal content. The sample of the study consisted of 300 music teachers working in 55 provinces at 6 grades, which were formed by taking into account the data of SEGE-2017. A questionnaire was applied to music teachers working in secondary schools in order to obtain quantitative data on accompaniment of songs with modal content. According to the results of the research; It has been determined that the majority of the music teachers accompany them in their lessons, and that the makams are generally recognized in accompaniment of songs with makam content and they can accompany these pieces
