Makale Başlığı: DURING THE COVID-19 PANDEMIC OVERVIEW OF THE DYNAMIC CHANGES IN THE GUIDELINES PUBLISHED BY THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH
Özet: The Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) pandemic caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus still continues to be a serious public health problem. In this study; 11 COVID-19 guidelines, which have undergone dynamical changes during the pandemic process in our country, have been compared in the historical process. These guides; prepared by the Scientific Committee, Ministry of Health of the Republic of Turkey was broadcast by the Directorate General of Public Health. The aim is to reflect the process in our country on the guidelines and to obtain a general perspective.
