Makale Başlığı: Opaka and Krepcha in the XV-XIX centuries (population, economy, cult centers)
Özet: Recently, the number of scientific publications in various languages based on Ottoman sources has increased all over the world. One of these publications is Opaka and Pancakes in the XV-XIXth (Population, Economy, Worship/Temple Centers)]. This study is the introduction of this mentioned book.
