Makale Başlığı: Blow up of solutions for a parabolic equation of Kirchhoff-type with multiple nonlinearities
Özet: In this paper, we investigated a class of doubly nonlinear parabolic systems with Krichhoff-type.We prove a blow up of solutions with negatif initial energy.
