Makale Başlığı: Manevi Danışmanlıkta İhsan Modeli
Özet: Spiritual Counseling is a discipline that is frequently used in countries such as Europe and America and on which a great number of research has been done. In our country, studies have started to be carried out in this field. In today's consulting models, there is a tendency towards holistic approaches. The Iḥsān Model is a unique religious counseling model integrated with the cognitive behavioral psychotherapy approach. The work named Iḥsān Model in Spiritual Counseling, offers a model proposal for spiritual counselors.
