Makale Başlığı: LAPAROSCOPIC CHOLECYSTECTOMY IN SITUS INVERSUS TOTALIS: A RARE CASE REPORT
Özet: Situs inversus totalis (SIT) is a rare clinical condition and is a mirror image transposition of the abdominal and thoracic organs. Cholelithiasis in situs inversus is very rare situation. Laparoscopic cholecystectomy is an effective and safe treatment method in patients with situs inversus. Surgeons need to be careful because of reverse anatomy and unconventional manual work. In this study, we aimed to present a case of cholelithiasis with situs inversus totalis in the light of literature.
