Makale Başlığı: THE STADIUM OF CLAUDIOPOLIS ANCIENT CITY
Özet: Stadiums, which are an integral part of the urban texture and urban identity from the Antiquity to the present, are monumental structures where sports events are held and allow the audience to watch these events. In this study, the importance of the Claudiopolis Stadium for the city, which is located in the Akpınar District of the central district of Bolu City, and whose rescue excavation was carried out by the Bolu Museum Directorate in 2008, was tried to be determined in the light of ancient inscriptions. In this context, the function and importance of the stadium structure in ancient times and its place in today's urban landscape are mentioned.
