Makale Başlığı: On the Characterisations of Curves with Modified Orthogonal Frame in $\mathbb{E}^{3}$
Özet: This study analyses (k,m)-type slant helices in compliance with the modified orthogonal frame in 3-dimensional Euclidean space ($\mathbb{E}^{3}$). Furthermore, we perform some characterisations of curves with modified orthogonal frames in $\mathbb{E}^{3}$.
