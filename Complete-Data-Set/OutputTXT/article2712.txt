Makale Başlığı: IRAN-IRAQ WAR AND İTS EFFECTS ON TURKEY
Özet: The 20th century has been a period in which very significant events took place in the world and the Iran-Iraq War which lasted for eight years was one of them and left a central mark on the Middle East balance. Border disputes, water problems, terrorism, and many other factors have been an effect on this war and it has caused economic and political deterioration. This article discusses the reasons and results of the Iran-Iraq War and its effects on Turkey in terms of its devastating outcomes even if Turkey did not take place in the war.
