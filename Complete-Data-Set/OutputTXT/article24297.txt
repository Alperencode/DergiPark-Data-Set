Makale Başlığı: Political Interest and Participation Effect of Political Science and Public  Administration Education: The Case of Süleyman Demirel University
Özet: Political participation is essential for democracy. Political participation is any political action taken by citizens to influence, directly or indirectly, the election of government officials and work they do. Political participation as a form of political behavior is influenced by many factors. Such factors such as age, gender, education at attainment, ethnicity, social status, the place where a person lives, level of organization, among others, are widely accepted as basic variables in the literature. This study aims to assess the impact of education in PSPA and the years spent at the university on the political interest and participation levels of the students of the Department of Political Science and Public Administration (PSPA). For this purpose, the questionnaire technique is used to measure comparatively the factors affecting the political interest and participation levels of 1st and 4th grade students. The data obtained were analyzed and evaluated according to the goals of this study with the help of the related statistical program
