Makale Başlığı: Arbitrability III Case of the German Federal Court of Justice
Özet: The standards governing the validity of arbitration agreement included in the contract of association and also involve disputes that challenge the shareholders' resolutions in limited liability company apply to partnerships such as the limited partnerships.
