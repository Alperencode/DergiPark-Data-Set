Makale Başlığı: Duygusal Zeka ve Dil Öğretimi
Özet: This paper reviews Emotional Intelligence (EI) and the development of models which focus on the individual's cognitive and non-cognitive abilities and skills. It also emphasizes the differences and similarities between the mentioned models and the value of EI in educational contexts.
