Makale Başlığı: Visual Selectivity Evaluations with Different Imaging Systems in Trawl Codends
Özet: In this study, 10 trawl operations weremade between November 2013 and April 2014 in the Iskenderun Bay. In theseopeartions, video and imaging records were carried out with systems modified inthe trawl hauling in the Iskenderun Bay. As a result of these studies, imageswere obtained in support of the selectivity studies in the region trawl fish.Modification underwater trawl cam used in these studies are more effective andless costly than commercial trawl cameras.
