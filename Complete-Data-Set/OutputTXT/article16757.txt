Makale Başlığı: DEVELOPMENT OF INTERNATIONAL RELATIONS DISCIPLINE  IN TURKEY
Özet: In this essay, the development of International Relations discipline, education of International Relations, studies of International Relations in Turkey are studied within two main parts. In the main parts important events related with discipline and their histories are studied. In the fourth part and the conclusion part, International Relations of Turkey in contemporary world and problems of discipline, is mainly analyzed.
