Makale Başlığı: Factors associated with severe lung disease in an adult population with cystic fibrosis: a single-center experience
Özet: Background/Aim: The patients with cystic fibrosis  CF  are living longer compared to the past, but respiratory failure is still the most   common cause of mortality. The aim of this study is to investigate factors associated with severe lung disease in a cohort of adult patients   with CF.   Materials and methods: Demographic data, clinical and laboratory findings of the patients aged 18 years and more were collected and   the patients were grouped according to forced expiratory volume in 1 s  FEV1     as severe group
