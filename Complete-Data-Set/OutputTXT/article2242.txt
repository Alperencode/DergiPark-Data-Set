Makale Başlığı: Pink and White Esthetic in Implant Supported Restoration
Özet: The aesthetic and functional success of implants, which are frequently preferred for replacing missing teeth, depends on the compatibility of soft and hard tissues with each other. In addition to its relationship with teeth, lips, gingiva and surrounding tissues, implant position, smile line, gingival emergency profile, form of alveolar bone are among the factors affecting this harmony. In this review, the aesthetic evaluation of hard and soft tissues around the implant is explained.
