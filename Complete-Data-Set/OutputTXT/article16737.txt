Makale Başlığı: Examination Of The Satisfaction Of Foreign Students Who Prefer Turkey For Education And Their Recommendation To The University: A Study At Kütahya Dumlupınar University
Özet: The aim of the research is to determine the relationship between university satisfaction and the intention to recommend the university of foreign students who prefer Turkey for education. For this purpose, the Personal Information Form is used to learn the demographic and educational information of 384 foreign students studying at Kütahya Dumlupınar University, the Satisfaction Scale is used to measure the university satisfaction of the students, and the Intention to Recommend Scale is used to determine the intention to recommend the university. As a result of the analysis of the data, a relationship was determined between the satisfaction of foreign students with the service provided by higher education institutions and their intention to recommend their universities to others.
