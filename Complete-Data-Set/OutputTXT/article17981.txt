Makale Başlığı: An Examination On Direction Signs Used İn Space Designs
Özet: Shopping malls are the junction of both architectural and graphic design, where various products and brands are found. It is important that how the preferred direction signs in shopping malls are applied in the space. The aim of the study is to reveal the semantic dimension added to the space by the column cladding, direction signs and floor applications in shopping malls.The study has carried out on three different award-winning shopping malls in Şişli. These are Trump Mall and Kanyon Mall. In this study the direction signs in shopping malls were examined in terms of typography, pictograms and color elements by using the qualitative research method in a holistic multi-state design. These shopping malls lead other buildings with the meaning they add to the spaces and the dimension they add to the architectural space design by revealing the care and experience they show in different areas.
