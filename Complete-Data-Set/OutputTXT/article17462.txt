Makale Başlığı: Spinoza ve Ethica Üzerine
Özet: Pierre Bayle’s “Spinoza” article in the Historical and Critical Dictionary provides a critical overview of the main issues in Spinoza's thought. But in the so called ‘Remark N’ he focuses mainly on the Ethics and presents a list of objections to the most fundamental concepts of Spinozian metaphysics such as mode, modification, substance, and God etc.
