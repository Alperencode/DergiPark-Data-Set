Makale Başlığı: A Review of Kaza Kumanova’s Demographic and Social Structure of XIX. Century
Özet: Kumanova, which at present is within the bordersof Macedonia, is an important city in the sense that it is a city in theBalkans which wtill carries traces from the Ottomans. It can be seen in thenineteenth century  dividend books that the Muslim and non-Muslimpopulation lived together in Kumanova,  a town in the province ofKöstendil which in the district of Skopje. It is possible to obtain dataregarding the population and social structure of towns, streets and villagesfrom the dividend books as social history source. In this article a socialanalyse of the area and the period has been tried to made in the light ofdividend books number 15377 and 15463 and by usage of individual names andnicknames.
