Makale Başlığı: The climate crisis and the global green new deal: The political economy of saving the planet
Özet: In this paper, I summarize and criticize the book titled "The Climate Crisis and The Global Green New Deal: The Political Economy of Saving the Planet" written by Robert Pollin and Noam Chomsky. This study consists of two chapters. The first one encompasses the synopsis of the book chapter by chapter. The second one includes a critique of the book.
