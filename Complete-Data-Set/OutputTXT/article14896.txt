Makale Başlığı: Imagination in Primary and Secondary Schools: What Do Teachers Think?
Özet: The purpose of this study was to identify the views of teachers working in primary and secondary schools on the role of school in the development of children’s imagination. This study utilised a qualitative case study approach. A semi-structured interview form developed by the researcher was used to collect data. The research was carried out in the first semester of the 2018-2019 academic year in Bursa Mustafakemalpaşa. The data collection process started with obtaining permissions and determining the schools.  11 volunteer teachers from 4 different schools participated in the data collection process. The data were analyzed using content analysis method. The results showed that the school supports the imagination of students but this support is limited. The participants associated the results to limited and standardized curriculum. As a result,  further research should be undertaken to investigate the role of school in the development of imagination by applying quantitative methods.
