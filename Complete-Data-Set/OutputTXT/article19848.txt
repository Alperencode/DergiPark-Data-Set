Makale Başlığı: The Potential Antiviral Activity of Inositol (Vitamin B8) as a Dietary Supplement in Human and Animal Nutrition
Özet: Inositol is categorized as an essential vitamin and recognised as a B8 vitamin. It and its derivatives including myo-inositol has some biological activity in several metabolisms. inositol is well tolerated and a natural constituent in human and animal nutrition. The toxicity of inositol is low. At the various concentrations, it presented antiviral activity against serious viruses such as rhinovirus, coxsackie, herpesvirus, HIV and iridovirus threatening biosafety, human and animal health. This review aimed to present the structure, role in nutrition, toxicity and potential antiviral activities of inositol and its derivatives as a dietary supplement in human and animal nutrition with One-Health concept.
