Makale Başlığı: A DESCRIPTIVE AND CRITICAL OVERVIEW OF THE RELATION BETWEEN GOD AND TIME
Özet: Abstract The concepts “time” and “God” were examined and the interpretations of different religions and cultures concerning these concepts were touched briefly within the scope of this study. The term “God”, whose nature can not be thought apart from His existence, was explained with the help of divine attributes subsequent to the views regarding to the concept “time” in the history of philosophy. The relation between God and time was questioned, the views concerning whether God is temporal, atemporal or omnitemporal were explained with their justifications and counter arguments.Keywords: Time, God, God’s Attributes, Temporality, Atemporality.
