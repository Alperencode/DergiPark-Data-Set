Makale Başlığı: ЭТНОЛОГИЧЕСКИЕ ПРЕДАНИЯ В КАРАКАЛПАКСКОЙ ПОЭЗИИ ХIХ ВЕКА  (НА МАТЕРИАЛЕ «ШЕЖИРЕ» («РОДОСЛОВНАЯ») БЕРДАХА
Özet: The article deal with the problems of usage of ethnological legends in the poem by Berdakh «Genelogy» («Shejire»). Legend connected with the origin of karakalpak tribes «kipchak», and their poetical embodiment by Berdakh have been analized in detail
