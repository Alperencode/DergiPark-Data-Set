Makale Başlığı: THE PROCESS OF PREPARING THE OTTOMAN EMPIRE FOR THE FIRST WORLD WAR: POLITICAL AND MILITARY CONJUNCTIVATION EXAMINATION (19th - 20th CENTURIES)
Özet: Ottoman Empire which had entered the challenging wars series at the beginnings of the first half of the19th century, have found itself in battlefield until 20th century while it has been leaving with defeatfrom some of this wars it has concluded others with victory. Otoman Empire which was desiring toenter into agreements that brings wars to an end, as a winner and by having advantageous in its hands,had wanted to constitute a revised army. Ottoman Empire that had applied the avant-garde reformmovements to the army firstley, had envisioned that the political success will come with the militarysuccess proportionally. This conception had been become as government policy by proceeding untilFirst World War.This study aims to examine the political processes and military evolutions which occured in OttomanEmpire from 19th century to First World War.
