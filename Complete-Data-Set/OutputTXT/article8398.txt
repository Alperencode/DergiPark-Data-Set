Makale Başlığı: Content Criticism Of Imam Adh-Dhahabī And The Usage Of The Term Munkar With A Reason In The Context Of Content Criticism
Özet: In this study, Zahabi's content criticism is examined through the book titled ez-Zehebî ve menhecuhû fî nakdi metni’l-hadîsi’ş-şerîf by Hasan Fevzî Hasan Saîdî. In addition, based on the examples in this book, the reasoned use of the term munkar within the scope of text criticism will be examined and its connection with content criticism will be tried to be better proven.
