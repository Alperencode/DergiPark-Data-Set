Makale Başlığı: OPINIONS ON BEGINNING LEVEL TUMPET TRAINING METHODS
Özet: The purpose of thıs study is to analyze the methods used in the teachıng of beginners trumpet using the opinions of educators in instıtutions providing professional musical education. The study was executed using databases, source scanning, and a survey administered on trumpet educators. The Crombah Alpha coefficient was determined as .929. Percentage (%), frequency (f), average (x̄) and standart deviation values of the obtained data were statistically interpreted through tables. ANOVA test was applied to determine whether the answers given by the lecturers differ according to the variables. At the and of the stdy, the most popular begınner level trumpet methods were designated, and the goals and behaviors of were determined according to the contents of the methods according to the opinions of the trumpet educators. At the same time analyzing the general specialties of the methods used in teaching beginner trumpet, information has been provided for trumpet educators and students.
