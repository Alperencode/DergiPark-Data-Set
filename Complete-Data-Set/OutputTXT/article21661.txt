Makale Başlığı: Wise City, Wise Place/Home concept
Özet: Each person to be viable, be sufficient place to live. Especially the pre-viable preterminfant, at the physiological essential, the requirements for life, must be designed fromthe individual basis. This is a medical tailoring according to the infant. Not so clearfundamentals, love and respectful at holding the life, not be at general perspective, justbe designed to preterm infants, thus, they are unique and sole.
