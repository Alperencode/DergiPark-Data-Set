Makale Başlığı: INVESTIGATION OF THE EFFECTS OF EMOTIONAL INTELLIGENCE, EMOTIONAL LABOR AND EGO STATES ON SERVİCE ORIENTATION: THE CASE OF HOSPITALITY BUSINESS
Özet: The aim of the study is to examine whether emotional intelligence, emotional labor and transactional analysis ego states have an effect on service orientation. Accordingly, a questionnaire was applied to 960 employees in 4 and 5 star hotels operating in Muğla. According to the results of the research, it has been determined that while others’ emotion appraisal and use of emotion dimension of emotional intelligence, deep acting and genuine acting dimension of emotional labor have positive effect on service orientation; surface acting dimension has a negative effect on service orientation. However, negative effects of critical parent and rebellious child ego states, positive and significant effect of nurturing parent ego states on service orientation were detected. Adult, free child and compliant child ego states did not have a significant effect on service orientation.
