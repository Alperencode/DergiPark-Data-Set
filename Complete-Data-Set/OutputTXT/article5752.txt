Makale Başlığı: The Structure and Theme in Refik Halid Karay’s Novel “Bugünün Saraylısı”
Özet: In the novel, Bugünün Saraylısı authored by Refik Halid Karay who is one of the significant writers of the national literature period of the Turkish literature, the effects of the modernization process at individual and societal levels are depicted. Within this process, the issues of corruption and belonging that come into being in view of getting distanced from cultural values arise as the main episodes that provide the element of conflict in the novel. Also, addressing individual emotions and inclinations such as love and jealousy besides individual and societal dissociation adds another dimension to the thematic configuration in the novel. Refik Halid Karay who presents these main themes from a critical perspective reflects his own worldview and lifestyle also in the novel, Bugünün Saraylısı, as in the case of his other literary works. Such that, the revelation of the importance and value of what is traditional by Refik Halid Karay, who narrates the idea that he wants the reader to receive and embrace through his characters and perceptions of time and space, carries traces of the reflections of his individual perspective. In this study, the novel, Bugünün Saraylısı, which sheds light on both the traditional life and the modern life, will be analyzed in terms of its structural elements as well as its entire content plane above.
