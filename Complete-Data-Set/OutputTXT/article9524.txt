Makale Başlığı: Difficulties Encountered by Syrian Students in Educational Activities
Özet: The aim of this study is to foreign students’ (Syrian students) opinions about the difficulties they encounter in their educational and training activities. For this purpose, a phenomenology model which is a qualitative research model was used since qualitative data were collected to resolve the problem. In this context to obtain qualitative data the study group was composed of 23 primary school students of public school who study in Arsuz which is a district in Hatay province. These students were selected according to criteria (Primary school students, Syrian students, Continuing education and training activities) determined by the researcher in advance using a purposeful sampling method. Among interview types, semi‐structured interview technique was used to create an answer for the problem in research. Through semi-structured interviews, students' views on the difficulties they experienced in educational activities were obtained Then, the obtained data were evaluated with content analysis.  As a result of the research, it was determined that the students had communication, adaptation and behavior problems mainly due to language inadequacy. With this study, the obtained results presented findings to reveal the situation and current deficiencies of foreign students living in our country in schools.
