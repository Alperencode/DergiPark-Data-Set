Makale Başlığı: Deli Dumrul in Dede Korkut
Özet: Many people are mentioned in Dede Korkut Epics that contain important information about Turkic history, geography, language, literature, culture, art and many more. One of them is Deli Dumrul. The name of Deli Dumrul is mentioned in one epic in The Book of Dede Korkut. Duha Koca Oglu Deli Dumrul Boyu has a sub-layer of Turkish history that goes back thousands of years to the depths of history. The struggle of Deli Dumrul with the Azrail in Dede Korkut is a reflection of the struggle of the Kams/Shamans in ancient Turks with evil spirits. There are many historical-epic-legendary-mythological layers, the oldest of which goes deep into history, in the type of Deli Dumrul, which has an important place in Dede Korkut. Duha Koca Oglu Deli Dumrul Boyu is a narrative that advises people not to be arrogant and virtuous, and contains important values and messages not only for Turks but for all humanity, justice and world peace. For this reason, it is impoartant for world literature as it is for Turkish literature. In this article, Deli Dumrul in Dede Korkut; his name, family, place in history, Dede Korkut and epics were examined.
