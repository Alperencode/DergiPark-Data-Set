Makale Başlığı: The Role And Importance Of Television Series In Türkiye’s Cultural Diplomacy Approach: A Field Research In Special Of Hungary
Özet: Public diplomacy is one of the main subjects of current political communication studies. In particular, the presence of governmental and non-governmental actors in international political communication activities has paved the way increased interest in public diplomacy studies. In Türkiye, according to the developments in the world, the value of public diplomacy studies has increased even more recently. One of the prominent sub-fields of public diplomacy, which has a wide application area, is cultural diplomacy.In order to contribute to Türkiye's cultural diplomacy studies is main target of this research. For this purpose, the study is so crucial in the way of uncovering the reflection of television series, which is one of the popular topics of discussion in Türkiye’s cultural diplomacy approach, to the audiences in Hungary. For this reason, the research was fulfilled in the cities of Budapest and Kaposvár, Hungary, amidst 20 December 2020 and 10 May 2021. Based on the purpose of the study, semi-structured interview technique was utilised in the research. Interviews with the participants were delimited to 17 attenders via snowball sampling technique. The data was collected through in-depth interviews and assayed with descriptive analysis. When the interviews conducted in English and Hungarian were analyzed, it was revealed that the TV series increased Türkiye's international visibility and created a positive perception, created virtual communities and contributed considerably to branding.
