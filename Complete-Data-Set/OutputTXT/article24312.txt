Makale Başlığı: The History of Parchment and Decorative Collections
Özet: Throughout history human beings had achieved processing leather with different techniques for different aims. Parchment have been processing generally from goat and sheep skins using lime and can carry writings on the both surfaces. It is rumored that the name of the parchment taken from Bergama. At this paper, history of parchment and samples of decorative usage have been given
