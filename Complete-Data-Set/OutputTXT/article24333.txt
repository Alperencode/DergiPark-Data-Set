Makale Başlığı: A Rare Case; Primary Cranial Hydatic Cyst
Özet: Hydatid cyst is a parasitic infection caused by Echinococcus ﻿granulosus larvae. Although liver and lung involvement is common, cranial involvement is very rare. Surgical removal of hydatid cysts without rupture is the main treatment. Therefore, preoperative diagnosis is very important.
