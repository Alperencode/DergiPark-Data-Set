Makale Başlığı: SOME STUDIES ABOUT “KHAKAS SHAMANISM/WORLD-WİWE”
Özet: In this article are discussed some studies done on “Khakas shamanism”. An examination of all the research on this topic is beyond the limits of this article. Therefore only some studies were examined. The investigation the books written about “Khakas shamanism” will require a major research. Some studies introduced in our research is undertaking “shamanistic worldview” in the folklore of Khakassia. Some research assesses also “practices of shamanic”. The purpose of this research is to provide information about “Hakas shamanism” the work done on. Thus, the informations about “Hakas shamanism” want to offer to the benefit of Turkish scientists slightly.  Keywords: Khakas, shamanism, world-wiwe, researchs.
