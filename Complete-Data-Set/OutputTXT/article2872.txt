Makale Başlığı: The Unknowns of Ottoman Cultural History: From Individuals to Works, From Institutions to Identities
Özet: In his book titled "The Unknowns of the History of Ottoman Culture: From Individuals to Works, From Institutions to Identities", Dr. Erünsal deals with previously unknown documents and some interesting issues related to our cultural history, which he identified during his studies on Old Turkish Literature and Ottoman libraries between 1980 and 2014. The book is a combination of updated versions of articles previously published in journals and books.
