Makale Başlığı: Defending the Unity of Wujud
Özet: Sufism; Its position among the sciences, its methodology in knowing the truths of things in the context of the shariah-truth relationship, and its interpretations of God-man and God-realm relationship have been discussed throughout history. Over time, these discussions have concentrated around the metaphysical understanding known as unity of existence, and many works have been written for and against. XVIII. In this book, which deals with the defense of unity of existence of Abdülgani Nâblusi (d. 1143/1731) who lived in the 19th century, the historical adventure and theoretical ground of the debates on unity of existence are discussed in summary and a concise but holistic perspective on the issue is tried to be presented.
