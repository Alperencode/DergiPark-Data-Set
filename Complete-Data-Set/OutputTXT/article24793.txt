Makale Başlığı: Partial anomalous pulmonary venous return anomaly: Case report
Özet: Partial anomalous pulmonary venous return anomaly (PPAVR) is a rare congenital anomaly, and it is defined as the drainage of one or more pulmonary vein to the right atrium or systemic circulation. We evaluated multidetector computed tomography angiography findings of a case with of partial anomalous pulmonary venous return.
