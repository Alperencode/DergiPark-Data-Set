Makale Başlığı: FINANCING MODELS OF THE OTTOMAN STATE FINANCE HISTORY
Özet: Finance models emerge as a result of efforts to find funds within economic systems. The 19th century and the second half of the Ottoman Empire was an era in which changes took place in economy and finance. In the study, in order to reveal the development process of financing models, ıt was investigated using descriptive analysis method the applications used by the Ottoman state in providing finance. In this context, financing models in the Ottoman economy were examined in connection with the Ottoman financial history. It will help to identify alternative finance models and applications by associating them with the current financial models understanding the situation in the historical process of thees models. In the study, first of all, ıt was evaluated the financial system and historical development process related to finance capital of the Ottoman state. Then, examined the way they are applied and financial models applied in this system. Finally, ıt was compared with the Islamic financial instruments used today the financing models of the Ottoman state.
