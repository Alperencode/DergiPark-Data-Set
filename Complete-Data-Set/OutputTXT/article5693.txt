Makale Başlığı: Molla Muhammed Ali and On His Poem Named “Safâ Geldin Hacı Baba”
Özet: Poets sometimes revealed their moods with the poems they wrote, and sometimes they translated the feelings of the society. These poems in question have been transferred from generation to generation, both orally and in writing, and have come to this day. This type of literature, which has been shaped around different subjects throughout history, continues its existence by preserving its originality in different geographies today. Palu district of Elazığ, which comes across as a home of science and wisdom with its scholars and mystical identity, is important in terms of the scholar poets it has raised from past to present. One of these personalities, who appear as both a scholar and a poet, is Molla Muhammed Ali.In addition to his religious and mystical identity, Molla Muhammed Ali still appears as a productive poet despite his advancing age. In this study, firstly, information will be given about the life of Molla Muhammed Ali. Afterwards, the translation and analysis of his poem named Safâ Geldin Hacı Baba will be made.
