Makale Başlığı: Examination of Left-Hand and Right-Hand Techniques in Viola Teaching
Özet: In order to achieve correct technical and musical results, it is important to apply the correct instrument techniques in a right way. The technical issues of the left and right hands in the viola are the main tools the performer will use to achieve these goals. Therefore, the aim of the research is; it was determined as the examination of right-hand and left-hand techniques in viola teaching. In the research, due diligence-based scanning model was used to examine left-hand and right-hand techniques in viola teaching, and the research was limited to the studies scanned in Google Scholar in the last twenty-five years. As a result of the research, suggestions were made for left-handed and right-handed techniques in viola teaching.
