Makale Başlığı: Turkey's Capitalist Economic System Transition Process As A Public  Policy Analysis
Özet: Capitalism, which became a dominant system in the western world after the feudalism of sona, quickly became a universal system with the historical process. Capitalism, perhaps in the 21st century, has entered the age of its most mature. On the basis of this, the historical transformations are based on political-economic practices.The role of the state varies from time to time depending on political-economic practices. When the state came to its place, it entered the market like a capitalist, and when it came to its place, it watched politics protecting and protecting capitalist.In the historical process, the state's permanent and fundamental task has been to make and implement regulations that will ensure the functioning of the capitalist system. In this direction, the development and conservation activity that will provide the function of capitalism is provided by the regulations made by formal government structures. Private property rights have been secured for the functioning of capitalism, and structural and functional regulations, such as the state's defense, security and law, have been rearranged to provide these conditions.Moreover, if the markets were not at the level of providing capitalism functionality, the state was created. In a similar situation occurred in the Republic of Turkey, together with the collapse of the Ottoman Empire, although the establishment of the capitalist system of capital accumulation and protection have taken the time, this process is supported by policies implemented by the government. At this point, this study aims to make an economic and political public policy analysis by examining the policies (the establishment of the capitalist system of production in Turkey, prepared for the development and operation) that and the process in question in the light of numerical data and laws
