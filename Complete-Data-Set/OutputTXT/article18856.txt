Makale Başlığı: A COMPARATIVE STUDY ON IMPLEMENTATION OF THE MINIMUM WAGE IN THE EUROPEAN UNION
Özet: The issue of wages is a multidimensional phenomenon in the middle of discussions as an indicator of individual and social development with economic, social and political aspects. The minimum wage, on the other hand, is an important issue of social policy, which forms an extension of the wage issue and affects it, often involving the state as a third party a longside the employer and employee. In the study, it is aimed to make a comparison of the implementation of the minimum wage within the European Union countries. In this respect, the subject of the minimum wage was defined in general, the formation of the minimum wage in the countries in question, the real value of the level differences, the effects of the economic crises on the minimum wages were evaluated. Qualitative-description method was used in the article by referring to written sources and various graphics.
