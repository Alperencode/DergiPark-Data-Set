Makale Başlığı: YAZINSAL ALANDA KLASİK ÜTOPYALAR VE TÜRK EDEBİYATI’NDA ÜTOPYA
Özet: A case of utopia; which produced mentally, willing to be occurred and reached but, is seen as imposible, which can be stated as a dream that built and produced in the mind to, constitude and reveal more optimal and beter government model or community structure and that can be seen in the sumerian’s clay tablets as a narrative become a craving for the human beings designing futurewith the desire of convergence and reach to a beter and beautiful life. In this study, primarily, some of the literary classical utopias will be evaluated, subsequently, short description about works which include utopian items in terms of Turkish literature and included items will be given. In the general evaluation and conclusion part, utopian structures will be discussed from different points of view
