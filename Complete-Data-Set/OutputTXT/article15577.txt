Makale Başlığı: Intuitionistic Fuzzy Hypersoft Separation Axioms
Özet: In the present paper, we introduce the notion of $T_{i}$ $(i=0,1,2,3,4)$ separation axioms in intuitionistic fuzzy hypersoft topological spaces and discuss some of its properties. By using this notions, we also give some basic theorems of separation axioms in intuitionistic fuzzy hypersoft topological spaces. Finally, we present hereditary property of intuitionistic fuzzy hypersoft topological spaces.
