Makale Başlığı: Method and Content Evaluation of "The Damascus Basin: Ulema and Scientific Life in Damascus in the Tenth Century"
Özet: Period and regional studies are at the forefront in terms of quantity in theology studies in Turkey. Ömer Elbeyli's work, which was prepared in 2018 as a doctoral thesis with the title of Ulema and Scientific Life in Damascus in the 4th century of AH and published under the name The Damascus Basin: Ulema and Scientific Life in Damascus in the Tenth Century in December 2021, also included Damascus selected as the region and the 4th/10th year selected as the period. In terms of being a century, it can be said that it covers a topic that is not emphasized much in Turkey.
