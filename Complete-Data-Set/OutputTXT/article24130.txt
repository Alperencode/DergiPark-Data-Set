Makale Başlığı: Evaluation of Coaches' Motivation Discourses
Özet: The purpose of this research is to examine the motivational discourses used by coaches in training, before, during and after the competition. Coaches at various levels and sports participated in the research. Interviews were conducted with the coaches through a semi-structured interview form. The collected data were analyzed by content analysis, which was evaluated under the qualitative research approach. The themes in the data were discovered. The coaches’ discourses were analyzed, and themes were determined, and each discourse was categorized under these themes. It has been observed that the coaches have a positive approach towards their athletes and are open to development and make motivational statements that improve technical and tactical skills.
