Makale Başlığı: Following Don Quixote’s Footsteps: A Comparative Approach to Elias Canetti’s Körleşme and Tahsin Yücel’s Sonuncu
Özet: In this study, the novels of Elias Canetti's Körleşme and Tahsin Yücel's Sonuncu, will be discussed in terms of characters and thematic similarities, and the jamming of the main characters in the novels between the world of the books and the world of phenomena will be examined. In particular, the relationship of the main characters of the novels with the books will be read with Don Quixote, one of the immortal figures of world literature. In the study, New Criticism method will be used.In the study, it was concluded that Canetti's Körleşme and Tahsin Yücel's Sonuncu may have been inspired by Don Quixote in terms of the intellectual's relationship with life and that both Cervantes, Canetti and Yücel wrote their works for similar purposes.
