Makale Başlığı: CURRENT DEVELOPMENTS IN TAX LAW
Özet: One of the effects of the coronavirus epidemic that has effected the world since the end of 2019 has been in the tax area. Evaluations about the tax measures taken and other tax developments and their effects on tax administration and taxpayers are included in this study.
