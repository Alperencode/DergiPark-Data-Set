Makale Başlığı: Superior vena cava syndrome in Behçet’s disease: case report
Özet: Vasculitis is a group of diseases characterized by inflammation in vascular structures. They are divided into small, medium, large and variable vasculitides. Behçet's disease is in the variable vasculitis group and can involve all vessels. Behçet's disease may cause superior vena cava syndrome. Genetic and environmental factors are thought to be effective, but the etiology is not known. It is closely associated with HLA-B51. It is more common in young adults and male gender. In this article, a 20-year-old male patient with Behçet's disease is presented.
