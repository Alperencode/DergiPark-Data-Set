Makale Başlığı: EFFECT OF TFRS 9 ON THE LEVEL OF LOAN LOSS PROVISIONS IN THE TURKISH BANKING SECTOR
Özet: The purpose of this study is to investigate the effect of the Turkish Financial Reporting Standard (TFRS) 9-Financial Instruments on the level of loan loss provisions in the Turkish banking system. In this context, the effect of TFRS 9 on mandatory and discretionary loan loss provisions is tested with data obtained from the annual financial statements and independent audit reports of 25 banks that had non-performing loan portfolios in 2017 and 2018. It is concluded that the amount of mandatory provisions, the ratio of provisions to total assets and, similarly, discretionary provisions increased significantly after the implementation of TFRS 9.
