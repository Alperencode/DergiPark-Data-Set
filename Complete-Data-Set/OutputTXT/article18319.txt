Makale Başlığı: Environmental Pollution
Özet: The environment, which is a physical, chemical, biological, social, economic and cultural medium, negativities arising as a result of industrialization, development of technology and population growth results are affected and the size of this effect is increasing rapidly day by day. Environmental pollution mainly occurs in nature in the form of air, soil and water pollution; It not only damages plant and animal existence, but also affects human health through the food chain and ultimately the entire ecosystem, including humans. Since the nature we live in has its own physical, chemical and biological properties, environmental pollution can be classified as physical, chemical and biological pollution.
